**Same-Day Vegan Pizza Dough (For Pizza Ovens)**

**Makes:** 3 balls (~285g each, perfect for 12" pizzas)

- **500g** Tipo 00 flour (or bread flour — higher protein is better for high heat)
- **335g** water (room temp, **67% hydration** — tighter dough holds up in rocket-hot ovens)
- **15g** fine sea salt
- **2g** active dry yeast (or 1.5g instant)
- **10g** sugar (optional but recommended for same-day — feeds yeast to speed fermentation. True Neapolitan omits sugar, but that needs 24+ hrs)

**Method:**
1. **Mix:** Whisk dry ingredients, add water, knead 2 mins until shaggy
2. **Rest 20 mins:** Cover (lets gluten relax)
3. **Knead again:** 2-3 mins until smooth
4. **Bulk rise:** 4-6 hours at room temp (longer if you're aiming for later — up to 12 hrs max)
5. **Divide & shape:** Split into 3 balls, round them tight
6. **Final proof:** 1-2 hours at room temp before baking

**Pizza Oven Tips:**
- Preheat stone **30 mins** at full blast
- Stone temp aim: **800-850°F** (900°F floor will burn the bottom)
- Bake time: **60-90 seconds**, turning as needed
- Don't stretch too thin — tears are impossible to fix when the cook is <2 min
