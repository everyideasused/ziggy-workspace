# Protein Trail Mix

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 5 min
**Servings:** 6 (1/2 cup per serving)
**Protein per Serving:** 20g
**Calories per Serving:** 320

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup raw almonds
- 1 cup raw walnuts (or pecans)
- 1 cup raw cashews
- 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
- 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
- 1/2 cup hemp seeds
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries (or raisins)
- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
- 1 tbsp sea salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

1. **Mix everything** — combine all nuts, seeds, dried fruit, chocolate chips in a large bowl
2. **Toss** — stir until evenly distributed
3. **Portion** — divide into 1/2 cup servings in small bags or containers
4. **Store** — airtight containers or freezer bags up to 1 month

## Nutrition (per serving)

| Macro | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| Calories | 320 |
| Protein | 20g |
| Carbs | 18g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Notes

- **Pre-workout snack** — eat one serving 30-45 min before training for sustained energy
- **Portable** — grab a bag, take anywhere
- **Balanced macro** — nuts/seeds for fat, fruit for quick carbs, hemp/seeds for protein
- **Shopping overlap:** Almonds, walnuts, cashews, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, hemp seeds
