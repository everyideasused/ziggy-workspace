## 📐 MEP Sanity Check

| Project Type | MEP as % of TI Hard Cost |
|---|---|
| Office TI | 30–40% |
| Retail TI | 28–38% |
| Restaurant (QSR) | 40–55% |
| Restaurant (FSR w/ bar) | 45–60% |
| Medical Office | 40–55% |
| Surgery Center / Clinical | 50–65% |

**If MEP < 28% on any commercial TI — something is missing. Audit before proceeding.**

## 🔑 Estimator's 10 Laws

1. Scope completeness beats unit cost precision
2. Form your own number before reviewing anyone else's
3. MEP is always 35–55% of TI — verify every time
4. Contingency is a quantified risk reserve, not a plug
5. Apply escalation to construction midpoint, not bid date
6. Every allowance will be spent; most will be exceeded
7. Utility connection fees are almost always missing — find them
8. A bid without 3 comparable bids is not a market check
9. Document assumptions so thoroughly the estimate can be rebuilt
10. Post-bid calibration is how you get better — track everything
