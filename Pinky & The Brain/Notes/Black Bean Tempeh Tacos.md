## 📋 Ingredients

### Taco Filling
- 8 oz tempeh (check for GF if needed)
- 3 tbsp taco seasoning (cumin, chili powder, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika)
- 1/3 cup water (for marinating)
- 1 small yellow onion, diced
- 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tbsp tamari (or soy sauce)
- 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 8 corn tortillas (or flour/whole grain)

### Toppings (Choose Your Favorites)

## 👨‍🍳 Instructions

1. **Marinate tempeh** – In a medium bowl, mix taco seasoning with 1/3 cup water. Crumble tempeh with your hands and add to seasoning. Stir well and set aside to marinate (few minutes).

2. **Prep aromatics** – Dice onion and mince garlic.

3. **Sauté onions** – In large pan over medium-high heat, sauté onion for 7-8 minutes until softened. *(Use 3 tbsp water/broth for oil-free method, adding more as needed.)*

4. **Add garlic mixture** – Reduce heat to medium. Add minced garlic, tomato paste, and tamari. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring often.

5. **Cook tempeh** – Add seasoned tempeh crumbles. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.

6. **Add beans** – Stir in black beans. Cook 4-5 minutes until heated through.

7. **Assemble tacos** – Warm tortillas if desired. Spoon 1/2 cup tempeh mixture down the middle of each tortilla. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

---

## 📝 Notes
> *Tweaks, substitutions, what worked, what didn't.*

- **Make it spicy** – Add chipotle in adobo sauce, cayenne pepper, jalapeños, or red pepper flakes to the filling
- **Other additions** – Bell pepper, poblano, zucchini, corn, mushrooms, tomatoes, cooked sweet potato all work great
- **Not just tacos** – Use filling for burritos, burrito bowls, rice bowls, taco salad, or nachos
- **Storage** – Keep crumbles in airtight container in fridge for 4-5 days. They taste even better as leftovers! Reheat in microwave.
- **Oil-free** – Use water/broth to sauté onions instead of oil. Use oil-free tortillas.
- **Gluten-free** – Check tempeh ingredients, use tamari, use corn tortillas
- **Yield** – Recipe makes about 4 cups taco crumbles = 8 small tacos with 1/2 cup filling each
