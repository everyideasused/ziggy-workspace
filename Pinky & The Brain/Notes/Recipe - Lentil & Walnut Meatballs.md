# Lentil & Walnut Meatballs

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Italian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 4 (4 meatballs each)
**Protein per Serving:** 44g
**Calories per Serving:** 580

---

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 1 cup cooked brown lentils (or 1 can, drained)
- 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped or pulsed
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- Black pepper to taste

### Sauce & Serving
- 24 oz marinara sauce
- 400g pasta (whole wheat or chickpea)
- Fresh basil for garnish
- Olive oil for baking

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** — 400°F
2. **Mix meatball dough** — combine lentils, walnuts, oat flour, nutritional yeast, soy sauce, garlic, balsamic, Italian seasoning, onion powder, pepper
3. **Form meatballs** — should hold together; if too dry, add 1 tbsp water at a time
4. **Shape** — 16 meatballs total
5. **Bake** — place on oiled baking sheet, bake 20-25 min until firm and slightly crispy
6. **Simmer in sauce** — heat marinara, add meatballs, simmer 5 min
7. **Cook pasta** — per package directions
8. **Serve** — pasta, meatballs, sauce, fresh basil

## Notes

- **Freezer hack** — freeze uncooked meatballs on a tray, transfer to bag, bake from frozen (add 5 min)
- **Walnut-lentil combo** — walnuts add texture and richness, lentils add protein
- **Flavor development** — balsamic and soy sauce create umami "meaty" depth
- **Shopping overlap:** Lentils, walnuts, oat flour, nutritional yeast, soy sauce
