# Protein Energy Balls

## Ingredients
- 1 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Mix oats, peanut butter, honey, protein powder, and vanilla extract
2. Fold in chocolate chips
3. Refrigerate for 15 minutes
4. Roll into 1-inch balls (makes about 24)
5. Store in airtight container in fridge for up to 2 weeks
6. Or freeze for up to 2 months

## Nutrition (per ball)
- Calories: 85
- Protein: 8g
- Carbs: 10g
- Fat: 4g
- Prep Time: 15 min
- Makes: 24 balls
