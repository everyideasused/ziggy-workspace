# Tempeh Taco Bowl

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Mexican
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 12 min | **Total:** 22 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 40g
**Calories per Serving:** 540

---

## Ingredients

- 450g tempeh, crumbled
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cups cooked black beans (or 1 can)
- 2 cups brown rice (cooked)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges
- Salsa

## Instructions

1. **Season tempeh** — mix soy sauce, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder
2. **Cook tempeh** — heat oil in skillet, crumble tempeh, add seasoning, cook 8-10 min until browned
3. **Warm beans** — heat black beans in microwave or stovetop
4. **Assemble bowls** — rice base, topped with tempeh, beans, tomatoes, onion, avocado
5. **Garnish** — cilantro, salsa, lime wedge

## Notes

- **Tempeh vs tofu:** Tempeh has more protein (19g per 100g), firmer texture, nuttier flavor
- **Meal prep:** Cook tempeh, portion into containers with separate toppings
- **Shopping overlap:** Tempeh, soy sauce, black beans, rice, lime used across recipes
