# CSI MasterFormat — Estimator Reference Index

> Full 50-division framework with unit cost ranges, scope triggers, and estimating notes.
> All costs in 2024 USD. Apply [[BM-Regional-Cost-Index]] multipliers.

---

## Division 00 — Procurement & Contracting

Not a cost division — contains bid documents, contracts, bonds.
- **Estimator Note:** Review Division 00 for bonding requirements, liquidated damages, retainage terms, and payment schedule. All affect GC pricing.

- Suburban/mid-market: $5,000–$30,000

## Division 11 — Equipment (Owner-Furnished / Coordinated)

**Estimator Note:** Division 11 is typically owner-furnished equipment (OFE/FFE). However, the GC scope must include:
- Rough-in and utilities for all OFE
- Blocking and backing for wall-mounted equipment
- Structural reinforcement for heavy equipment
- Coordination with equipment submittals
- Final connections (often included in MEP)

**Restaurant-specific OFE coordination budget:** $15,000–$40,000 for coordination/infra not in equipment package

## Excluded Items (Always Clarify)

These are commonly excluded from GC scope and must be tracked:
- Architect/Engineer fees
- Owner's project manager fees
- Permit fees (even if GC pulls permit)
- Utility connection/development fees
- Testing and balancing (sometimes included, verify)
- Commissioning (Cx)
- FF&E (furniture, fixtures, equipment if OFE)
- Brand-standard proprietary items
- Technology (POS, drive-thru, AV — may be by owner)
- Signage (often by owner or separate sign contractor)
- Security monitoring service (infra is in scope, monitoring is not)
