# Protein-Packed Buddha Bowl

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** American / Bowl
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 10 min
**Servings:** 1
**Protein per Serving:** 46g
**Calories per Serving:** 680

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1/2 block (200g) baked tofu, cubed
- 1/2 cup roasted chickpeas
- 1 cup mixed greens (kale, spinach, arugula)
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- 1 cup shredded beets and carrots
- 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
- 1/4 cup raw hemp seeds
- 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- 1/4 cup sprouted lentils (or canned lentils)

### Dressing (Tahini-Lemon)
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 1.5 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- 4 tbsp water
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Make dressing** — whisk tahini, lemon juice, garlic, maple syrup, water, salt, pepper
2. **Assemble bowl** — quinoa base, arranged sections of tofu, chickpeas, greens, veggies
3. **Top with seeds** — hemp seeds and pumpkin seeds
4. **Drizzle dressing** — pour tahini dressing over top
5. **Eat immediately** or pack for lunch (dressing on side)

## Nutrition (per serving)

## Notes

- **Protein breakdown** — baked tofu (20g) + chickpeas (12g) + hemp seeds (10g) + quinoa (8g) = 50g+ when properly portioned
- **Dinner powerhouse** — one bowl hits all macros for lean bulk
- **Prep tip** — cook grains Sunday, chop veggies, assemble fresh
- **Shopping overlap:** Quinoa, tofu, chickpeas, tahini, hemp seeds staples
