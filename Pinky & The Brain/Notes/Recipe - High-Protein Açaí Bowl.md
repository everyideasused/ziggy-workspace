# High-Protein Açaí Bowl

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** Brazilian
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 5 min
**Servings:** 1
**Protein per Serving:** 30g
**Calories per Serving:** 490

---

## Ingredients

### Base
- 2 frozen açaí packets (200g)
- 1 scoop (30g) vegan protein powder (vanilla or unflavored)
- 1/2 frozen banana
- 1/4 cup soy milk

### Toppings
- 2 tbsp hemp seeds
- 2 tbsp granola
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup mixed berries
- 1 tbsp coconut flakes

## Instructions

1. **Blend base** — açaí packets, protein powder, banana, soy milk — blend until thick (should be ice cream consistency)
2. **Pour into bowl** — should be thick enough to hold toppings
3. **Arrange toppings** — layer hemp seeds, granola, almond butter, berries, coconut
4. **Eat immediately** — melts fast

## Notes

- **Key trick:** Minimal liquid = thick bowl (not a smoothie)
- **Protein comes from:** protein powder (24g) + hemp seeds (6g)
- **Post-workout option:** Great for post-morning-workout refueling
- **Shopping overlap:** Protein powder, hemp seeds, soy milk staples
