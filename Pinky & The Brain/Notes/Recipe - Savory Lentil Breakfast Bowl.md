# Savory Lentil Breakfast Bowl

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** Indian-Inspired
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 20 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Servings:** 3
**Protein per Serving:** 28g
**Calories per Serving:** 400

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
- 2 cups water
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 cups baby spinach
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. **Cook lentils** — bring lentils and water to boil, reduce heat, simmer 15 min until tender
2. **Meanwhile, sauté** — heat coconut oil, cook onion 3 min, add garlic 1 min
3. **Spice it** — add cumin, turmeric, garam masala, stir 30 seconds
4. **Add tomatoes** — cook 2 min until softened
5. **Combine** — add cooked lentils to the spice mixture
6. **Wilt spinach** — stir in spinach until wilted
7. **Season** — salt, pepper, squeeze of lemon
8. **Serve** — garnish with cilantro

## Notes

- **Makes 3 servings** — eat one, fridge two for quick reheats
- **Freezer-friendly:** Freezes perfectly for up to 3 months
- **Serve with:** Naan, rice, or just a spoon
- **Red lentils** cook fastest (no soaking needed) — perfect for weekday mornings
- **Shopping overlap:** Red lentils, spinach, spices used across multiple recipes
