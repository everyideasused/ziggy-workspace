# Chickpea & Vegetable Stir-Fry

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Asian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Servings:** 3
**Protein per Serving:** 40g
**Calories per Serving:** 540

---

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 cans (800g total) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp sriracha (optional)
- 3 cups cooked brown rice
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 4 green onions, sliced

## Instructions

1. **Make sauce** — whisk soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, sriracha
2. **Heat wok** — sesame oil over medium-high, cook garlic and ginger 30 seconds
3. **Add chickpeas** — stir-fry 2 min
4. **Add veggies** — broccoli 3 min, pepper and peas 2 min, mushrooms 1 min
5. **Pour sauce** — toss everything, simmer 2 min until veggies are tender-crisp
6. **Serve** — over brown rice, top with sesame seeds and green onions

## Notes

- **Chickpea advantage** — canned, no cooking required, ready to use
- **Protein sources** — chickpeas (27g) + brown rice (15g) = 40g+ total
- **Meal prep** — stir-fry base and rice separate, combine fresh or reheat together
- **Shopping overlap:** Chickpeas, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil, brown rice staples
