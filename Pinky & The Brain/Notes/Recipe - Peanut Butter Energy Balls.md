# Peanut Butter Energy Balls

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 10 min (+ 30 min chill)
**Servings:** 12 balls
**Protein per Serving:** 22g
**Calories per Serving:** 260

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup natural peanut butter
- 1 scoop (30g) vegan protein powder (vanilla or chocolate)
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 1/4 cup ground flaxseed
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder (optional, or omit)
- Pinch of sea salt
- Optional: 2 tbsp dark chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. **Mix all ingredients** — combine peanut butter, protein powder, maple syrup, oat flour, flaxseed, cocoa, salt
2. **Stir until combined** — should be thick and moldable
3. **Scoop** — use a 1.5 tbsp scoop or small ice cream scoop, make 12 balls
4. **Chill** — refrigerate 30 min minimum
5. **Store** — airtight container in fridge (up to 1 week) or freezer (up to 3 months)
6. **Eat** — straight from fridge/freezer, naturally frozen for slow-melting chew

## Notes

- **Pre-workout snack** — eat one 30 min before workout for quick carbs + protein
- **Freezer hack** — eat frozen for natural "ice cream" texture
- **Flavor swap** — chocolate version with cocoa, or plain PB version
- **Shopping overlap:** Peanut butter, protein powder, oat flour, flaxseed staples
