# White Bean Minestrone

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Italian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 4
**Protein per Serving:** 28g
**Calories per Serving:** 420

---

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cans (800g total) white beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 1 cup green beans, chopped
- 1 cup pasta (small shapes)
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish
- Lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. **Sauté aromatics** — heat oil, cook onion 3 min, add garlic, carrot, celery 3 min
2. **Add broth and tomatoes** — bring to boil
3. **Add white beans** — stir in
4. **Add pasta and green beans** — cook 8-10 min until pasta is tender
5. **Wilt spinach** — stir in spinach
6. **Season** — Italian seasoning, nutritional yeast, salt, pepper
7. **Serve** — hot, with fresh basil and lemon

## Notes

- **Makes 4 servings** — soup is king for meal prep
- **Freezer** — up to 4 months; pasta gets soft when frozen, add fresh pasta when reheating
- **White beans** — mild flavor, creamy texture, 19g protein per can
- **Shopping overlap:** Beans, vegetables, broth, pasta staples
