# Overnight Protein Oats

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 5 min (+ overnight)
**Servings:** 1
**Protein per Serving:** 35g
**Calories per Serving:** 480

---

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 scoop (30g) vegan protein powder (vanilla)
- 1 cup soy milk
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1/2 cup mixed berries (topping)

## Instructions

1. **Combine** — mix oats, protein powder, soy milk, chia seeds, maple syrup in a jar or container
2. **Stir well** — make sure protein powder is fully incorporated (no clumps)
3. **Refrigerate** — cover and place in fridge overnight (min 4 hours)
4. **Morning** — stir, top with almond butter and berries
5. **Eat cold or warm** — microwave 90 seconds if you prefer warm
