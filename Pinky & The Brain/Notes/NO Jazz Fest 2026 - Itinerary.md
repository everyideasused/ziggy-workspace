# New Orleans Jazz Fest Trip — April 22-27, 2026

**Trip with:** Cassandra 
**Focus:** New Orleans Jazz & Heritage Festival + additional activities  
**Duration:** 6 days (arrival Wed 4/22, departure Monday 4/27)

---

## Quick Facts

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Jazz Fest Dates** | April 23-26 (Weekend 1) + April 30-May 3 (Weekend 2) at Fair Grounds |
| **Tickets** | 4-day weekend pass ~$319 + fees; single-day ~$100+ (on sale in March) |

## Day-by-Day Itinerary

### **Wednesday, April 22 — Arrival**
- **Afternoon:** Fly MSY, check into hotel
  - Suggested: French Quarter (walkable to everything) or Marigny/Bywater (artsy, live music)
- **Dinner:** [[Meals from the Heart Cafe]] — vegan beignets + gumbo
- **Evening:** Frenchmen Street jazz crawl (free live music, no cover charges)
  - Try: The Spotted Cat, Snug Harbor, Frenchmen Street bars

### **Thursday, April 23 — Jazz Fest Day 1**
- **Morning–Evening:** Fair Grounds (opens ~10am)
  - **Strategy:** Mix headline stages with smaller "Jazz Tent" for authentic NOLA jazz
  - **Lunch:** Fair Grounds vendors (scout vegan options)
  - **Pro tip:** Download mobile app for schedule, artist bios, venue map
- **Dinner:** [[Seed]] — tofu po'boy + vegan gumbo
- **Evening:** Bourbon Street OR Frenchmen Street jazz

### **Friday, April 24 — Jazz Fest Day 2 + City Exploration**
- **Morning–Afternoon:** Fair Grounds (hit stages you missed yesterday)
  - Catch artist interviews/panels throughout day
- **Lunch:** [[G's Tasty Pizza]] — vegan pizza + rotating vegan raw cakes
- **Afternoon Date Activity:** [[New Orleans Botanical Garden]] (City Park) — romantic, photos, peaceful
- **Dinner:** [[Sneaky Pickle]] (Bywater) — farm-to-table, excellent cocktails, vegan options
- **Evening:** Live music crawl (2–3 different jazz clubs)

### **Saturday, April 25 — Jazz Fest Day 3 + Neighborhoods**
- **Morning:** Fair Grounds (final day of Weekend 1)
- **Afternoon:** Leave by 2pm, explore neighborhoods
  - **French Quarter:** Royal Street, antiques, Cathedral Square
  - **Marigny/Bywater:** Art galleries, vintage shops, street art
  - **Garden District:** Mansions, tree-lined streets, romantic walk
- **Dinner:** [[Parkway Poor Boys]] — vegan Beyond Burger (local classic, casual)
- **Evening:** Rooftop bar or Bourbon Street area

### **Sunday, April 26 — Jazz Fest Final + Relaxation**
- **Morning:** Optional Jazz Fest (early sessions only)
- **Afternoon:** Leave Fair Grounds by 2pm
- **Activity:** Mississippi Riverfront walk or [[Audubon Park]] sunset picnic (wine + peaceful)
- **Dinner:** Date night — [[Bouligny Tavern]] (cocktails) + [[Lilette]] (dinner) — romantic classic

### **Monday, April 27 — Off-Fest Exploration**
- **All day:** Explore without Jazz Fest crowds
  - Frenchmen Street jazz (weekend wrap-up)
  - Optional: Presbytere Museum (Mardi Gras history), New Orleans Jazz Museum
  - Food crawl: hit vegan spots you haven't tried
- **Evening:** Dinner + low-key live music spot

## Pro Tips

1. **Jazz Fest pacing:** Don't see everything — pick 5–6 must-sees, build itinerary around those
2. **Footwear:** Wear very comfy shoes (Fair Grounds = miles of walking)
3. **Hydration:** April heat + humidity — constant water + sunscreen
4. **Crawfish season:** Peak in April — try at least once
5. **Vegan options:** Better than you'd expect; most spots have genuine options, not afterthoughts
6. **Music discovery:** Catch unknown artists on smaller stages — find gems
7. **Between weekends:** Don't force Jazz Fest on April 27 if tired — just explore the city
8. **Parking:** Use Uber/public transit; street parking in French Quarter is nightmare fuel
