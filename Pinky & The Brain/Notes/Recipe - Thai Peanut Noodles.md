# Thai Peanut Noodles

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Thai
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 12 min | **Total:** 22 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 32g
**Calories per Serving:** 520

---

## Ingredients

- 200g rice noodles or soba noodles
- 300g baked tofu (or extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed)
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp peanut butter
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/4 cup water
- Red pepper flakes to taste
- Fresh cilantro and lime wedges
- 2 tbsp crushed peanuts

## Instructions

1. **Cook noodles** — per package directions, drain and set aside
2. **Make sauce** — whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, sesame oil, water, garlic, ginger
3. **Blanch veggies** — broccoli 3 min, drain (or use raw if prefer crunch)
4. **Assemble** — noodles, tofu, veggies in a bowl
5. **Add sauce** — pour peanut sauce over, toss gently
6. **Garnish** — crushed peanuts, cilantro, lime wedge, red pepper flakes

## Notes

- **Baked tofu** — pre-made and flavored, saves cooking time
- **Meal prep:** Noodles and sauce separate; assemble fresh or eat cold next day
- **Flavor boost:** Fresh lime juice, cilantro, hot sauce essential
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, peanut butter, soy sauce, sesame oil, broccoli
