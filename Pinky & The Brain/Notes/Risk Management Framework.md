# Risk Management Framework

> Cross-cutting reference for [[Construction PM Knowledge Base]]

---

## RAID Log Structure

| Element | Definition | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **R**isk | Uncertain event that may impact the project | Material price escalation; permit delay |
| **A**ction | Task assigned to someone with a deadline | Submit RFI #45 by Friday; schedule pre-app meeting |
| **I**ssue | Risk that has materialized — needs resolution now | Structural steel delivery delayed 3 weeks |
| **D**ecision | Choice that needs to be made (with options and impact) | Proceed with VE option A or B? |

---

## Risk Categories by Phase

### Phase 1-2 (Pipeline & Feasibility)
- Site not suitable (environmental, geotech, zoning)
- Market conditions change (interest rates, demand)
- Deal falls through (seller, landlord)
- Due diligence costs on failed deals

### Phase 3 (Design)
- Scope creep / owner indecision
- Coordination errors between disciplines
- Design exceeds budget
- Code interpretation disagreements

### Phase 4 (Permitting)
- Extended plan review timelines
- Code compliance issues requiring redesign
- Public opposition (for entitled projects)
- Impact fees higher than anticipated

### Phase 5-6 (Procurement & Preconstruction)
- Insufficient bid coverage (not enough bidders)
- Bids exceed budget
- Subcontractor qualification issues
- Long-lead item delays
- Bonding capacity limitations

### Phase 7 (Construction)
- Unforeseen site conditions
- Weather delays
- Material supply chain disruption
- Subcontractor performance/default
- Safety incidents
- Change order disputes
- Quality deficiencies
- Labor shortages

### Phase 8-9 (Closeout & Warranty)
- Punch list dragging
- Missing closeout documents
- Retainage disputes
- Latent defects discovered post-occupancy
- Warranty claim disputes

## Risk Response Strategies

| Strategy | Description | Example |
|----------|------------|---------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk entirely | Don't pursue a contaminated site |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce probability or impact | Order long-lead items early to reduce delay risk |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to another party | Performance bond; insurance; contractual risk allocation |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge and budget for it | Owner contingency for unforeseen conditions |

---

## Contingency Guidelines

| Project Phase | Recommended Contingency |
|--------------|------------------------|
| Conceptual estimate (Phase 1-2) | 15-25% |
| Schematic Design estimate | 10-15% |
| Design Development estimate | 7-10% |
| Construction Document estimate | 5-7% |
| GMP (contractor contingency) | 3-5% |
| Owner contingency (during construction) | 5-10% |
