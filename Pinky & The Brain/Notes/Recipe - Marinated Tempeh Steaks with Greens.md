# Marinated Tempeh Steaks with Greens

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** American / Steak House
**Prep Time:** 10 min + 30 min marinating | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** 55 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 48g
**Calories per Serving:** 620

---

## Ingredients

### Tempeh Steaks
- 450g tempeh block, sliced lengthwise into 4 thick steaks
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 tsp thyme
- Black pepper to taste

### Sides
- 4 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, kale)
- 8 asparagus spears
- 200g baby potatoes, halved
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. **Marinate tempeh** — steam tempeh 10 min first (removes bitterness), slice, toss with soy sauce, balsamic, maple syrup, oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme; marinate 30 min minimum
2. **Roast potatoes** — toss with oil, salt, pepper; roast at 425°F for 25 min
3. **Pan-sear tempeh** — heat skillet over medium-high, sear steaks 5-6 min per side until caramelized and crispy
4. **Roast asparagus** — toss with oil, add to potatoes for last 10 min of cooking
5. **Wilt greens** — toss in a bit of oil with salt and pepper; warm for 2 min
6. **Plate** — greens base, tempeh steaks on top, roasted veggies on the side
7. **Finish** — drizzle with balsamic glaze

## Notes

- **Steakhouse feel** — feels fancy, actually simple to execute
- **Tempeh** — steam first = removes funky flavor, much better marinade absorption
- **Meal prep** — marinate in morning, cook when home; veggies can be prepped
- **Shopping overlap:** Tempeh, soy sauce, garlic, oil used across recipes
