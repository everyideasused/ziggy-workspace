# Soba Noodle & Edamame Salad

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Japanese
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 8 min | **Total:** 18 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 30g
**Calories per Serving:** 420

---

## Ingredients

- 200g soba noodles (buckwheat)
- 1.5 cups frozen edamame, thawed
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 4 green onions, sliced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- 3 tbsp sesame seeds
- Red pepper flakes to taste
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. **Cook soba** — per package directions (usually 4-5 min), drain and rinse with cold water
2. **Make dressing** — whisk soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, maple syrup
3. **Combine** — soba noodles, edamame, carrots, cucumber, green onions in a bowl
4. **Add dressing** — toss well
5. **Top** — sesame seeds, cilantro, red pepper flakes
6. **Chill** — best served cold

## Notes

- **Soba** — buckwheat noodles are 100% complete protein; edamame adds 11g
- **Meal prep** — assemble in containers, dressing on side, dress fresh day-of
- **Shopping overlap:** Soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds staples
