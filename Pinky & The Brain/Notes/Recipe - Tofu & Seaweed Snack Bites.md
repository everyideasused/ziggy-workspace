# Tofu & Seaweed Snack Bites

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** Asian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 20 min | **Total:** 30 min
**Servings:** 12 bites
**Protein per Serving:** 26g
**Calories per Serving:** 240

---

## Ingredients

- 400g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 4 sheets nori (seaweed), cut into squares
- Cooking spray

## Instructions

1. **Marinate tofu** — mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, maple syrup
2. **Add tofu cubes** — toss to coat, marinate 15 min minimum
3. **Preheat oven** — 400°F
4. **Arrange on sheet** — single layer on parchment paper
5. **Bake** — 20 min, shaking pan halfway through, until edges are crispy
6. **Cool slightly** — wrap each cube with a small piece of nori
7. **Sprinkle** — sesame seeds and red pepper flakes
8. **Serve** — warm or room temperature, store in airtight container

## Notes

- **Umami bomb** — soy sauce + ginger + garlic = deeply satisfying
- **Freezer** — baked bites freeze well, reheat in oven 5 min
- **Meal prep** — make Sunday, eat all week
- **Seaweed hack** — wraps while warm, will crisp as it cools
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds, nori
