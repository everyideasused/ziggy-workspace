# Lentil Bolognese

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Italian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 4
**Protein per Serving:** 32g
**Calories per Serving:** 480

---

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 celery stalk, diced
- 1.5 cups brown or green lentils, rinsed
- 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (200ml) tomato paste
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 400g pasta (whole wheat or chickpea)
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions

1. **Sauté aromatics** — heat oil, cook onion 3 min, add garlic, carrot, celery 3 min
2. **Add lentils** — stir in lentils, toast 1 min
3. **Add liquids** — diced tomatoes, tomato paste, broth
4. **Simmer** — bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 20 min until lentils are tender
5. **Season** — oregano, basil, nutritional yeast, salt, pepper
6. **Cook pasta** — per package directions
7. **Serve** — sauce over pasta, garnish with fresh basil

## Notes

- **Makes 4 servings** — freeze 3, eat 1 fresh
- **Flavor develops** — even better the next day
- **Freezer** — up to 4 months; reheat on stovetop with splash of water
- **Shopping overlap:** Lentils, onions, garlic, carrots, celery, tomatoes, pasta staples
