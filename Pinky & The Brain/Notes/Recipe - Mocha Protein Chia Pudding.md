# Mocha Protein Chia Pudding

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 5 min (+ 4h chill)
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 28g
**Calories per Serving:** 380

---

## Ingredients

- 6 tbsp chia seeds
- 1.5 cups soy milk
- 1 scoop (30g) chocolate vegan protein powder
- 1 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 shot espresso or 1 tsp instant coffee dissolved in 2 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tbsp hemp seeds (topping)
- Cacao nibs (topping, optional)

## Instructions

1. **Mix base** — whisk soy milk, protein powder, cocoa powder, espresso, maple syrup
2. **Add chia seeds** — stir thoroughly to avoid clumps
3. **Stir again at 10 min** — chia tends to clump, stir once more
4. **Refrigerate** — cover and chill minimum 4 hours or overnight
5. **Serve** — divide into two portions, top with hemp seeds and cacao nibs

## Nutrition (per serving)

| Macro | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| Calories | 380 |
| Protein | 28g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 18g |
| Fiber | 16g |

## Notes

- **Coffee + protein** — breakfast and caffeine in one
- **Batch prep:** Makes 5 servings in mason jars for the week
- **Keeps 5 days** in fridge
- **Texture tip:** More chia = thicker, more milk = thinner
- **Shopping overlap:** Chia seeds, soy milk, protein powder, hemp seeds
