# Tuesday Conditioning + Pull — Phase 1

**Focus:** Cardiovascular endurance, pull-up skill work, active recovery  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Equipment:** Running route, pull-up bar, resistance bands

---

## Session Structure

**Part A:** Walk/Run Progression (20 min)  
**Part B:** Pull-up Skill Work (7 min)  
**Part C:** Cool-down (3 min)

---

## Part A: Walk/Run Progression (20 min)

**Goal:** Build aerobic base, progress from walking → continuous running over 12 weeks

### Weeks 1-2: Base Building
**Format:** 20 min easy walk  
**Effort:** Conversational pace, nose breathing  
**Focus:** Build habit, no intensity

### Weeks 3-4: Walk + Jog Intervals
**Format:** 30 sec jog / 90 sec walk × 10 rounds (20 min total)  
**Effort:** Jog at easy effort (60-70% max HR)  
**Focus:** Movement quality, breathing rhythm

### Weeks 5-6: Increase Work Intervals
**Format:** 60 sec jog / 60 sec walk × 10 rounds (20 min total)  
**Effort:** Jog at steady effort (65-75% max HR)  
**Focus:** Consistent pacing, nasal breathing during walk

### Weeks 7-8: Build Aerobic Base
**Format:** 90 sec jog / 30 sec walk × 10 rounds (20 min total)  
**Effort:** Jog at moderate effort (70-75% max HR)  
**Focus:** Rhythmic breathing, relaxed shoulders

### Weeks 9-10: Near-Continuous Running
**Format:** 3 min jog / 1 min walk × 5 rounds (20 min total)  
**Effort:** Jog at controlled effort (70-80% max HR)  
**Focus:** Form economy, foot strike

### Weeks 11-12: Continuous Running
**Format:** 20 min continuous easy jog  
**Effort:** Conversational pace (70-75% max HR)  
**Focus:** Endurance, mental toughness

**End of Phase 1 Benchmark:** 20 min continuous jog without walking

---

## Part B: Pull-up Skill Work (7 min)

**Goal:** Build pull-up strength and endurance through greasing the groove

### Skill Block (7 min, repeat every 90 sec):
Perform 4-5 rounds of:

**1. Scapular Pull-ups** — 5 reps  
- Dead hang, engage shoulders (pull shoulder blades down)  
- No elbow bend, just scapular movement  
- Hold top for 2 sec

**2. Negative Pull-ups** — 3 reps  
- Jump to top position (chin over bar)  
- Lower slowly for 5 seconds  
- Fully extend arms at bottom

**3. Band-Assisted Pull-ups** — 3-5 reps  
- Use resistance band under one foot  
- Full range of motion (dead hang → chin over bar)  
- Control both up and down

**4. Dead Hang** — 15-20 seconds  
- Active hang (shoulders engaged, not relaxed)  
- Build grip endurance

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: Focus on scapular pulls + negatives (build foundation)
- Week 5-8: Add band-assisted pulls, reduce band thickness as able
- Week 9-12: Mix strict pull-ups (as many as possible) + band-assisted to failure

## Part C: Cool-down (3 min)

2. **Standing Quad Stretch** — 30 sec per leg  
   *Purpose:* Hip flexor + quad release

3. **Downward Dog** — 60 sec  
   *Purpose:* Hamstring stretch, shoulder mobility, calf stretch

## Tips for Success

**Running Form:**
- Midfoot strike, not heel
- Cadence: 170-180 steps/min
- Relaxed shoulders, arms at 90°
- Look ahead, not down

**Pull-up Skill Work:**
- Quality > quantity
- Full range of motion
- Rest as needed between rounds
- Track progress (band thickness, reps, hang time)

**Recovery:**
- This is an active recovery day
- Don't push to exhaustion
- Focus on movement quality
- Eat/hydrate well after
