# Red Lentil & Spinach Dal

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Indian
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 30 min
**Servings:** 3
**Protein per Serving:** 40g
**Calories per Serving:** 480

---

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
- 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
- 3 cups vegetable broth
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Serve with brown rice or flatbread

## Instructions

1. **Sauté aromatics** — heat oil, cook onion 3 min, add garlic and ginger 1 min
2. **Add spices** — curry powder, turmeric, cumin, garam masala; stir 30 seconds
3. **Add lentils** — toast 1 min in the spices
4. **Add liquids** — diced tomatoes, broth; bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 15 min
5. **Add spinach** — stir in spinach, cook 2 min until wilted
6. **Finish** — stir in coconut milk, lime juice, nutritional yeast
7. **Season** — salt, pepper to taste
8. **Serve** — garnish with cilantro, with brown rice or flatbread on side

## Notes

- **Red lentils** — cook fastest, break down into creamy texture
- **Protein powerhouse** — lentils (21g) + spinach (10g) + nutritional yeast (8g) = 39g+
- **Freezer** — up to 3 months, thaw and reheat on stovetop
- **Flavor development** — day-old is even better, flavors meld
- **Shopping overlap:** Red lentils, coconut oil, onions, garlic, ginger, spices, spinach
