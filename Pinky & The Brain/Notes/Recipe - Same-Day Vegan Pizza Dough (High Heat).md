# Same-Day Vegan Pizza Dough (High Heat Ready)

Perfect for pizza ovens running at 800-900°F. This same-day dough develops enough flavor and strength for high-heat Neapolitan-style pizzas without overnight fermentation.

## Overview

- **Yield:** 3 dough balls (~285g each, makes 12" pizzas)
- **Total Time:** 8-10 hours (minimal hands-on time)
- **Optimized For:** Pizza ovens at 800-900°F
- **Hydration:** 67%

## Ingredients

- 500g Tipo 00 flour
- 335g room temperature water (67% hydration)
- 15g fine sea salt
- 2g active dry yeast (or 1.5g instant yeast)
- 10g sugar (optional but recommended for same-day speed)

## Method

### 1. Mix Dry Ingredients
- Combine flour, salt, yeast, and sugar (if using) in a large bowl
- Mix thoroughly to distribute yeast evenly

### 2. Add Water & Initial Knead
- Add room temperature water
- Mix until no dry flour remains
- Knead for 2 minutes until roughly combined

### 3. Rest (Autolyse)
- Cover bowl with damp towel
- Let rest 20 minutes (allows gluten to develop naturally)

### 4. Final Knead
- Knead dough for 2-3 minutes until smooth and elastic
- Dough should be soft but not sticky

### 5. Bulk Fermentation
- Form into a ball and place in lightly oiled bowl
- Cover with damp towel
- Let rise at room temperature for 4-6 hours
- Dough should roughly double in size

### 6. Divide & Shape
- Turn dough onto lightly floured surface
- Divide into 3 equal portions (~285g each)
- Shape each portion into a tight ball
- Place balls on lightly floured tray with space between

### 7. Final Proof
- Cover balls with damp towel
- Let proof at room temperature for 1-2 hours before baking
- Dough should be puffy and relaxed (easier to stretch)

## Pizza Oven Tips

### Preparation
- **Preheat:** Heat pizza oven for 30 minutes at full blast
- **Stone Temperature:** 800-850°F is ideal
- **Flour Surface:** Use Tipo 00 flour on peel to prevent sticking

### Shaping
- Don't stretch dough too thin — leave a thicker edge for the cornice
- Work gently to preserve air bubbles
- Stretch from center outward, rotating as you go

### Baking
- **Bake Time:** 60-90 seconds total
- **Rotation:** Rotate pizza 180° halfway through (30-45 seconds)
- **Watch Closely:** High heat means fast cooking — don't walk away!

### Doneness Indicators
- Cornice (edge) should be puffed and leopard-spotted
- Bottom should have dark brown spots but not be charred
- Cheese should be bubbling

## Storage

- **Room Temperature:** Use within 2 hours of final proof for best results
- **Refrigerator:** Can refrigerate shaped balls for up to 24 hours; bring to room temp 1-2 hours before baking
- **Freezer:** Freeze shaped balls wrapped in plastic for up to 3 months; thaw overnight in fridge, then bring to room temp

## Notes

- **Vegan Status:** 100% vegan — no dairy, eggs, or animal products
- **Sugar's Role:** Provides food for yeast to speed same-day fermentation; can be omitted for longer ferments
- **Hydration:** 67% is a sweet spot — high enough for extensibility, low enough to handle easily
- **Flour Choice:** Tipo 00 is ideal for high heat (creates crispier, airier crust)

## Variations

### Herbs & Garlic
Add to dough during final knead:
- 1 tbsp dried Italian herbs
- 2 cloves minced garlic

### Whole Wheat
Substitute up to 100g Tipo 00 with whole wheat flour (reduce water slightly if too sticky)

### Sourdough Version
Replace yeast with 100g active sourdough starter; increase bulk fermentation to 6-8 hours
