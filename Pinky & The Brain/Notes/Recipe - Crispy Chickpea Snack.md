# Crispy Chickpea Snack

## Ingredients
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and dried
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Pat chickpeas dry with paper towels
3. Toss with olive oil and seasonings
4. Spread on baking sheet
5. Roast for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through
6. Cool completely before storing
7. Keep in airtight container for up to 5 days

## Nutrition (per serving, 1/4 cup)
- Calories: 110
- Protein: 10g
- Carbs: 12g
- Fat: 5g
- Prep Time: 25 min
- Serves: 4
