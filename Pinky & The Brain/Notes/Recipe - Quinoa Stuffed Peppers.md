# Quinoa Stuffed Peppers

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** American / Mediterranean
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 40 min
**Servings:** 2 (2 peppers each)
**Protein per Serving:** 44g
**Calories per Serving:** 520

---

## Ingredients

- 4 large bell peppers (any color)
- 2 cups cooked quinoa (1 cup uncooked)
- 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
- 1 red onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup diced tomatoes (fresh or canned)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 2 tbsp hemp seeds
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** — 375°F
2. **Prepare peppers** — cut off tops, remove seeds, lightly oil the outside
3. **Sauté filling base** — heat oil, cook onion 3 min, add garlic 1 min
4. **Combine filling** — mix in cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, tomatoes, cumin, chili powder, cilantro, salt, pepper
5. **Stuff peppers** — divide filling among peppers, pack gently
6. **Bake** — in baking dish with 1/4 cup water, cover with foil, bake 25 min
7. **Top** — remove foil, sprinkle hemp seeds, bake 5 more min
8. **Serve** — hot or room temperature

## Notes

- **Make 4, freeze 2** — assemble, wrap in foil, freeze; thaw overnight, bake cold version takes 35 min
- **Protein balance** — quinoa (13g) + black beans (15g) + hemp seeds (8g) + veggies = 44g
- **Filling variations** — lentils, chickpeas, pinto beans all work
- **Shopping overlap:** Quinoa, black beans, corn, tomatoes, hemp seeds
