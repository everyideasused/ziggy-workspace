# Falafel Wrap with Tahini Sauce

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Middle Eastern
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** 30 min
**Servings:** 2 (3 falafels each)
**Protein per Serving:** 34g
**Calories per Serving:** 480

---

## Ingredients

### Falafel
- 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/4 cup parsley
- 1/4 cup cilantro
- 2 tbsp chickpea flour
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp coriander
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- Salt and pepper
- Oil for frying

### Tahini Sauce
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp water
- Salt to taste

### Wraps
- 2 whole wheat tortillas or flatbreads
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1/2 cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 tomato, diced
- 1/4 red onion, sliced

## Instructions

1. **Make falafel dough** — pulse chickpeas, onion, garlic, herbs, flour, spices until grainy
2. **Form patties** — shape into 6 patties (about 2 inches wide)
3. **Fry** — heat oil over medium-high, fry 3-4 min per side until golden
4. **Make tahini sauce** — whisk tahini, lemon juice, garlic, water, salt
5. **Assemble wraps** — spread tahini sauce on tortilla, add lettuce, cucumber, tomato, onion
6. **Add falafel** — place 3 falafels on each wrap
7. **Roll and serve** — wrap tightly or eat with hands

## Notes

- **Falafel freezer hack** — make double batch, freeze uncooked patties, fry from frozen (add 2 min)
- **Flavor bomb** — tahini sauce is key; don't skip it
- **Shopping overlap:** Chickpeas, tahini, lemon, garlic, spices used across recipes
