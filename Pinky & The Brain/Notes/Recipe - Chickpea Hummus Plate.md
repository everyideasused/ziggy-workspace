# Chickpea Hummus Plate

## Ingredients
- 1 cup hummus
- 1 whole grain pita bread
- 1 cup carrot sticks
- 1 cup celery sticks
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 cucumber, sliced
- 1/4 cup roasted chickpeas
- Olive oil for drizzling
- Fresh herbs (mint or parsley)

## Instructions
1. Transfer hummus to a plate or bowl
2. Drizzle with olive oil
3. Sprinkle with roasted chickpeas
4. Arrange pita and vegetables around the hummus
5. Garnish with fresh herbs
6. Serve at room temperature
