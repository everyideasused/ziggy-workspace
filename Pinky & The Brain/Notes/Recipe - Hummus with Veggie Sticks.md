# Hummus with Veggie Sticks

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** Middle Eastern
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 10 min
**Servings:** 4 (1/4 cup hummus + veggie sticks)
**Protein per Serving:** 18g
**Calories per Serving:** 240

---

## Ingredients

### Hummus
- 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- Pinch of paprika
- Salt to taste
- 2-3 tbsp water (as needed for consistency)

### Veggie Sticks
- 2 carrots, cut into sticks
- 1 cucumber, cut into sticks
- 1 bell pepper, cut into strips
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1 cup snap peas

## Instructions

1. **Make hummus** — add chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, paprika to food processor
2. **Blend** — 60 seconds until smooth (add water 1 tbsp at a time if too thick)
3. **Season** — taste, adjust lemon juice and salt
4. **Transfer to bowl** — drizzle a bit of olive oil on top, sprinkle paprika
5. **Prep veggies** — cut fresh, arrange around hummus
6. **Serve immediately** — or store hummus separate from veggies

- **Hummus keeps** — in airtight container 5-7 days
- **Protein powerhouse** — chickpeas (14g) + tahini (4g) = 18g per serving
- **Pre-competition snack** — easy on stomach, sustained energy, zero digestive stress
- **Shopping overlap:** Chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon, olive oil staples
