# Thai Green Curry

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Thai
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 3
**Protein per Serving:** 38g
**Calories per Serving:** 520

---

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 3 tbsp Thai green curry paste (check vegan)
- 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 400g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- Fresh basil for garnish
- 3 cups cooked jasmine rice

## Instructions

1. **Heat coconut oil** — medium-high heat
2. **Bloom curry paste** — add curry paste, stir-fry 1 min to release flavors
3. **Add liquids** — pour in coconut milk and broth slowly, stirring to combine
4. **Add proteins** — tofu and chickpeas, simmer 5 min
5. **Add veggies** — broccoli 5 min, bell pepper 3 min, snap peas 2 min
6. **Season** — garlic, ginger, lime juice, soy sauce, maple syrup; taste and adjust
7. **Serve** — over jasmine rice, garnish with fresh basil

## Notes

- **Curry paste** — read label, some contain fish sauce; look for vegan options
- **Tofu + chickpeas** — redundant protein, but gives great texture variety
- **Freezer** — up to 3 months; freeze without rice, reheat on stovetop with splash of water
- **Shopping overlap:** Coconut oil, coconut milk, tofu, chickpeas, curry paste, jasmine rice
