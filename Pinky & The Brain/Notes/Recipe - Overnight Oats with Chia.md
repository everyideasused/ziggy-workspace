# Overnight Oats with Chia

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup oats
- 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 banana (sliced)
- 1 tbsp almond butter

## Instructions
1. In a mason jar, combine oats, almond milk, yogurt, protein powder, and chia seeds
2. Stir well to combine
3. Cover and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours)
4. In the morning, stir and add more milk if needed
5. Top with banana and almond butter

## Nutrition
- Calories: 320
- Protein: 22g
- Carbs: 35g
- Fat: 10g
- Prep Time: 5 min (+ overnight)
- Serves: 1
