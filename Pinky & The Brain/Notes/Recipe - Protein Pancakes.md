# Protein Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Toppings: berries, almond butter, maple syrup

## Instructions
1. Blend protein powder, eggs, oats, yogurt, baking powder, vanilla, and salt until smooth
2. Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat
3. Pour batter into 3-inch circles
4. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden
5. Top with berries and almond butter
