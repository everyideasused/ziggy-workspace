# PB Banana Protein Smoothie

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 5 min
**Servings:** 1
**Protein per Serving:** 38g
**Calories per Serving:** 520

---

## Ingredients

- 1 scoop (30g) vegan protein powder (pea/rice blend)
- 2 tbsp peanut butter (natural, no added sugar)
- 1 large banana (frozen for thickness)
- 1 cup soy milk (unsweetened)
- 2 tbsp hemp seeds
- 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
- 1/2 cup ice
- Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Add liquids first** — pour soy milk into blender
2. **Add solids** — banana, peanut butter, protein powder, hemp seeds, flaxseed
3. **Add ice** — for thickness
4. **Blend 60 seconds** — until smooth and creamy
5. **Adjust** — add more soy milk if too thick
6. **Pour and drink** — best consumed immediately

## Nutrition (per serving)

| Macro | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| Calories | 520 |
| Protein | 38g |
| Carbs | 42g |
| Fat | 24g |
| Fiber | 8g |

## Notes

- **Lean bulk essential** — easy 520 cal and 38g protein in 5 min
- **Frozen banana** is key — makes it thick like a milkshake
- **Swap PB for almond butter** for variety
- **Add oats** (1/3 cup) for extra 150 cal if needed
- **Shopping overlap:** Soy milk, hemp seeds, flaxseed used across multiple recipes
