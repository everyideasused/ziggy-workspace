## Monday, March 10 — First Workout! 🎉

**Workout:** [[Nathan 170@12 — Monday Lower]]  
**Time:** 30 minutes  
**Focus:** Learn movements, go light, perfect form

### The Plan

**Warm-up (5 min):**
1. Cat-Cow × 10
2. Hip Circles × 10 each direction
3. Bodyweight Squat × 10
4. Glute Bridge × 15
5. Light KB Deadlift × 10 @ 6 kg

**Main Work (20 min):**

**A1. Goblet Squat**
- 3 sets × 8 reps @ 6 kg
- Rest 90 seconds
- **Focus:** Full depth, elbows inside knees, chest up

**A2. Kettlebell Swing**
- 3 sets × 15 reps @ 8 kg
- Rest 90 seconds
- **Focus:** Hip hinge (NOT squat), explosive snap, arms are ropes

**B. Bulgarian Split Squat**
- 3 sets × 8 reps per leg (bodyweight only)
- Rest 60 seconds between legs
- **Focus:** Vertical front shin, drive through heel

**C. Single-Leg Deadlift**
- 2 sets × 8 reps per leg (bodyweight only)
- Rest 60 seconds between legs
- **Focus:** Hip hinge, back leg straight, hips square

**Finisher (5 min):**
- Farmer Carry: 3 sets × 30 sec per side @ 8 kg
- Rest 60 seconds between sides

**Cool-down (3 min):**
1. Couch Stretch × 60 sec/leg
2. Pigeon Pose × 60 sec/leg
3. Forward Fold × 60 sec
