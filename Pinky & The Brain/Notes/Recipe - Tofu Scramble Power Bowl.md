# Tofu Scramble Power Bowl

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Total:** 15 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 32g
**Calories per Serving:** 420

---

## Ingredients

- 1 block (400g) extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
- 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp black salt (kala namak) — gives eggy flavor
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Hot sauce (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Press tofu** — wrap in paper towels, press for 5 min (or use tofu press)
2. **Heat oil** in large skillet over medium-high heat
3. **Crumble tofu** into pan with hands — keep some chunks for texture
4. **Season** — add turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, nutritional yeast, black salt
5. **Cook 5 min** — stirring occasionally until tofu starts to brown
6. **Add veggies** — toss in bell pepper and black beans, cook 3 min
7. **Wilt spinach** — add spinach, stir until wilted (1 min)
8. **Season** — salt, pepper, hot sauce to taste
9. **Serve** — divide between two bowls

## Notes

- **Meal prep:** Makes great burritos — wrap in tortillas and freeze
- **Protein boost:** Add 2 tbsp hemp seeds on top (+10g protein)
- **Pairs with:** Toast, avocado, or hash browns for extra calories on bulk
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, nutritional yeast, black beans used in multiple recipes
