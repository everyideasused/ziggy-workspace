# Crispy Tofu Stir-Fry Bowl

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** Asian
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 36g
**Calories per Serving:** 520

---

## Ingredients

- 500g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp coconut oil (for frying)
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 2 cups cooked brown rice

## Instructions

1. **Press tofu** — wrap in paper towels, press 10 min or use press
2. **Make sauce** — whisk soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, garlic, ginger, sesame oil
3. **Heat wok** — medium-high, add 1 tbsp coconut oil
4. **Crisp tofu** — fry cubes 4-5 min per side until golden (work in batches), set aside
5. **Cook veggies** — add remaining oil, stir-fry broccoli 3 min, add pepper and peas 2 min
6. **Combine** — return tofu to wok, pour sauce over, toss gently 1-2 min
7. **Serve** — over brown rice, top with sesame seeds

## Notes

- **Crispy trick:** Don't move tofu much while frying — let it brown
- **Meal prep:** Make 3 batches Sunday, eat all week
- **Freezer-friendly:** Freeze without rice, reheat and serve over fresh rice
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil, brown rice staples
