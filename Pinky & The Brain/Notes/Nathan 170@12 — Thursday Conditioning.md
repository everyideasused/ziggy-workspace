# Thursday Conditioning + Mobility — Phase 1

**Focus:** Active recovery, mobility work, movement skill practice  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Equipment:** Walking route, yoga mat, resistance bands (optional)

---

## Session Structure

**Part A:** Walk/Run Progression (20 min)  
**Part B:** Mobility Flow (7 min)  
**Part C:** Skill Work (3 min)

---

## Part A: Walk/Run Progression (20 min)

**Same progression as Tuesday — see [[Nathan 170@12 — Tuesday Conditioning]]**

### Weeks 1-2: Base Building
20 min easy walk

### Weeks 3-4: Walk + Jog Intervals
30 sec jog / 90 sec walk × 10 rounds

### Weeks 5-6: Increase Work Intervals
60 sec jog / 60 sec walk × 10 rounds

90 sec jog / 30 sec walk × 10 rounds

3 min jog / 1 min walk × 5 rounds

### Weeks 11-12: Continuous Running
20 min continuous easy jog

## Part B: Mobility Flow (7 min)

**Goal:** Improve range of motion, address tightness, prevent injury

**Complete 2 rounds of the following flow:**

### 1. Cat-Cow — 10 reps (30 sec)
- On hands and knees
- Inhale: arch back, look up (cow)
- Exhale: round spine, tuck chin (cat)
- Smooth, controlled movement

### 2. World's Greatest Stretch — 3 per side (60 sec)
- Lunge position, back knee down
- Elbow to inside of front foot (hip opener)
- Rotate torso, reach arm to sky (thoracic rotation)
- Return to start, switch sides

### 3. Deep Squat Hold — 45 sec
- Bodyweight squat, as deep as possible
- Hold bottom position
- Elbows inside knees, chest up
- Rock side to side gently

### 4. Hip Flexor Stretch — 30 sec per side (60 sec)
- Lunge position, back knee on mat
- Push hips forward
- Optional: reach arms overhead
- Feel stretch in front of back hip

### 5. Thoracic Bridge — 5 reps (30 sec)
- Lie face down, hands by shoulders
- Push up into cobra/upward dog
- Hips stay on ground
- Look up, open chest

### 6. 90/90 Hip Stretch — 30 sec per side (60 sec)
- Sit with both knees at 90° (one internal, one external rotation)
- Lean forward over front leg
- Switch sides

## Part C: Skill Work (3 min)

**Choose ONE skill to practice each session (rotate weekly):**

### Option 1: Handstand Progression (Weeks 1-4)
- Wall walk-up × 3 attempts
- Wall hold (chest to wall) × 20-30 sec × 3 sets
- Hollow body hold × 20 sec × 3 sets (prerequisite)

### Option 2: Pistol Squat Progression (Weeks 5-8)
- Assisted pistol (hold pole/TRX) × 5 per leg × 3 sets
- Box pistol (lower to box, stand) × 5 per leg × 3 sets
- Single-leg balance × 30 sec per leg

### Option 3: L-Sit Progression (Weeks 9-12)
- Tucked L-sit × 10-20 sec × 3 sets
- One-leg extended L-sit × 5-10 sec per leg × 3 sets
- Support hold (parallel bars or floor) × 20-30 sec × 3 sets

## Tips for Success

**Mobility Work:**
- Never force a stretch
- Breathe deeply, relax into positions
- Hold stretches 30-60 sec for adaptation
- Focus on areas that feel tight from strength work

**Skill Work:**
- This is low-intensity practice, not maximal effort
- Focus on movement quality and body awareness
- Rest as needed
- Film yourself for form feedback

**Recovery Goals:**
- Leave this session feeling BETTER than when you started
- If you're sore/tired, spend more time on mobility and less on skill work
- This session prepares you for Friday's workout
