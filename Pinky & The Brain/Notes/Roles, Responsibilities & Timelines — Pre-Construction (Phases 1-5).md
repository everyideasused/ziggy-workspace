# Roles, Responsibilities & Timelines — Pre-Construction (Phases 1-5)

> Operational playbook for [[Construction Program Management - Master Reference]]
> See also: [[Roles, Responsibilities & Timelines — Construction & Closeout (Phases 6-10)]]

---

## How to Read This Document

Each phase is broken into a **week-by-week (or milestone-based) task sequence** showing:
- **What** needs to happen
- **Who** is responsible (R), accountable (A), consulted (C), or informed (I)
- **Duration** benchmarks
- **Critical handoffs** between parties

### Key to Stakeholder Abbreviations
| Abbrev | Role |
|--------|------|
| OWN | Owner / Developer / Client |
| PM | Program Manager / Owner's Rep |
| RE | Real Estate Broker |
| ATT | Attorney (real estate or construction) |
| ARC | Architect of Record |
| ENG | Engineers (structural, MEP, civil) |
| CM | Construction Manager (agency or CMAR) |
| GC | General Contractor |
| SUB | Subcontractors |
| AHJ | Authority Having Jurisdiction |
| LL | Landlord (if leased property) |
| LND | Lender / Financial Institution |
| ENV | Environmental Consultant |
| GEO | Geotechnical Engineer |
| SUR | Surveyor |
| EST | Cost Estimator |

## Phase 1: Real Estate Pipeline & Site Selection

> **Typical Duration:** 2-12 months (ongoing for rollout programs)
> **See:** [[01 - Real Estate Pipeline & Site Selection]]

### Task Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1-2 | Define site criteria (size, demographics, budget, brand requirements) | PM | OWN | RE | — |
| 1-4 | Market analysis and site identification | RE | RE | OWN, PM | — |
| 2-6 | Site tours and evaluation against criteria | RE, PM | OWN | ARC (if needed) | — |
| 3-6 | Preliminary zoning pre-screen | RE, ATT | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 4-8 | LOI drafting and negotiation | RE, ATT | OWN | PM | — |
| 4-8 | Preliminary budget development (order of magnitude) | PM, EST | PM | ARC, CM | OWN |
| 5-10 | Lease / purchase agreement negotiation | ATT | OWN | RE, PM | — |
| 5-10 | TIA negotiation (if leased) | RE, ATT | OWN | PM, EST | — |
| 8-12 | Execute lease or purchase contract | ATT | OWN | RE, PM | All |
| 8-12 | Authorize due diligence expenditures | OWN | OWN | PM | ATT |

### Critical Handoffs
- **RE → PM:** Site package (demographics, photos, preliminary zoning info) transferred at LOI
- **ATT → PM:** Executed lease/contract with all exhibits (Work Letter, TIA terms) delivered
- **OWN → PM:** Authorization to spend on due diligence and design

### Key Decisions by Owner
1. Approve site selection
2. Approve budget parameters
3. Approve lease/purchase terms including TIA
4. Authorize due diligence spend

## Phase 2: Feasibility & Due Diligence

> **Typical Duration:** 4-12 weeks
> **See:** [[02 - Feasibility & Due Diligence]]

### Task Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Engage environmental consultant for Phase I ESA | PM | PM | ENV | OWN |
| 1 | Engage geotechnical engineer | PM | PM | GEO | OWN |
| 1 | Engage surveyor for ALTA survey | PM | PM | SUR | OWN |
| 1 | Begin AHJ research for jurisdiction | PM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 1-2 | Order title commitment | ATT | ATT | PM | OWN |
| 1-4 | Phase I ESA fieldwork and report | ENV | ENV | PM | OWN |
| 1-4 | Geotechnical borings and report | GEO | GEO | PM, ENG | OWN |
| 2-4 | ALTA/NSPS survey | SUR | SUR | PM | OWN, ARC |
| 2-4 | Zoning analysis and compliance memo | ATT, ARC | PM | OWN | — |
| 2-6 | Utility capacity confirmation (written) | PM, ENG | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 3-6 | Preliminary construction budget | EST, CM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 3-6 | Financial proforma development | OWN, PM | OWN | ATT, LND | — |
| 4-8 | Phase II ESA (if triggered by Phase I) | ENV | ENV | PM | OWN |
| 4-8 | Existing conditions assessment (renovations) | ARC, ENG | PM | GC/CM | OWN |
| 6-10 | Compile all DD reports; prepare Go/No-Go memo | PM | PM | All consultants | OWN |
| 8-12 | **Owner Go/No-Go Decision** | OWN | OWN | PM, ATT | All |

### Critical Handoffs
- **ENV → PM:** Phase I report (and Phase II if applicable) — triggers environmental risk assessment
- **GEO → ENG:** Geotech report — structural engineer needs this to design foundations
- **SUR → ARC:** ALTA survey — architect needs this to begin site design
- **PM → OWN:** Go/No-Go recommendation with all supporting documentation

### Key Decisions by Owner
1. Proceed/abandon based on environmental findings
2. Proceed/abandon based on geotech findings
3. Accept/reject financial proforma
4. **Formal Go/No-Go decision** — commits to design expenditure
5. Authorize architect/engineer engagement

### Timeline Dependencies (What Blocks What)
```
Phase I ESA ──────► Go/No-Go Decision
Geotech Report ───► Structural Design (Phase 3)
ALTA Survey ──────► Site Design (Phase 3)
Zoning Analysis ──► Architectural Design (Phase 3)
Utility Confirms ─► MEP Design (Phase 3)
Title Commitment ─► Closing / Lease Execution
```

## Phase 3: Design — SD, DD, CD

> **Typical Duration:** 8-48 weeks depending on complexity
> **See:** [[03 - Design — SD, DD, CD]]

### Sub-Phase 3A: Schematic Design (SD)

**Duration:** 2-12 weeks

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Kick-off meeting: program review, site review, schedule | ARC | PM | OWN, ENG | All |
| 1 | Architect receives survey, geotech, zoning, existing conditions | PM | PM | — | ARC, ENG |
| 1-2 | Owner provides program requirements, brand standards, equipment list | OWN | OWN | PM | ARC |
| 1-3 | Conceptual site plan development (2-3 options) | ARC | ARC | ENG (civil) | PM, OWN |
| 2-4 | Conceptual floor plans (2-3 options) | ARC | ARC | OWN | PM |
| 2-4 | Preliminary code analysis (occupancy, egress, accessibility) | ARC | ARC | — | PM |
| 3-5 | Owner review and option selection | OWN | OWN | PM | ARC |
| 4-6 | Refined SD based on owner feedback | ARC | ARC | ENG | PM, OWN |
| 4-6 | Exterior elevation concepts | ARC | ARC | OWN | PM |
| 5-8 | Preliminary cost estimate (±20-30%) | EST, CM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 6-8 | SD presentation to owner | ARC | PM | OWN | — |
| 7-10 | **Owner approves SD, authorizes DD** | OWN | OWN | PM, ARC | All |

### Sub-Phase 3B: Design Development (DD)

**Duration:** 3-16 weeks

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | DD kick-off; all engineering disciplines engaged | ARC | PM | ENG (all) | OWN |
| 1-3 | Detailed floor plans with dimensions | ARC | ARC | OWN | PM |
| 1-4 | Structural system design (preliminary) | ENG (struct) | ENG | ARC | PM |
| 2-5 | MEP system design (schematics) | ENG (MEP) | ENG | ARC | PM |
| 2-5 | Material and finish selections | ARC | ARC | OWN | PM |
| 3-6 | Interior design and finish schedule | ARC | ARC | OWN | PM |
| 3-6 | Building section and wall section development | ARC | ARC | ENG | PM |
| 4-8 | Discipline coordination meetings (weekly) | ARC | PM | ENG (all) | OWN |
| 5-8 | Preliminary equipment/long-lead identification | CM, GC | PM | ARC, ENG | OWN |
| 6-10 | Updated cost estimate (±10-15%) | EST, CM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 6-10 | Value engineering review (if over budget) | CM, ARC | PM | OWN, ENG | — |
| 8-12 | DD presentation to owner | ARC | PM | ENG | OWN |
| 8-12 | **Landlord design review submission** (if leased) | PM | PM | ARC | OWN, LL |
| 10-14 | **Owner approves DD, authorizes CD** | OWN | OWN | PM, ARC | All |
| 10-14 | GMP pricing begins (if CMAR) | CM | CM | ARC, PM | OWN |

### Sub-Phase 3C: Construction Documents (CD)

**Duration:** 3-20 weeks

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1-8 | Complete all architectural drawing sheets | ARC | ARC | — | PM |
| 1-8 | Complete structural drawings | ENG (struct) | ENG | ARC | PM |
| 1-8 | Complete MEP drawings | ENG (MEP) | ENG | ARC | PM |
| 1-6 | Complete civil/site drawings | ENG (civil) | ENG | ARC | PM |
| 2-8 | Write specifications (Project Manual) | ARC | ARC | ENG | PM |
| 4-8 | Inter-discipline coordination review | ARC | PM | ENG (all) | — |
| 6-10 | QC review (internal to design team) | ARC | ARC | ENG | PM |
| 6-10 | Final cost estimate (±5-10%) | EST, CM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 8-12 | Assemble permit application package | ARC | PM | — | OWN |
| 8-12 | GMP finalization (if CMAR) | CM | CM | ARC, PM | OWN |
| 8-12 | **Owner final CD review and approval** | OWN | OWN | PM, ARC | All |
| 10-14 | **Submit for building permit** | PM | PM | ARC | OWN, AHJ |
| 10-14 | Release bid documents (if DBB) | PM | PM | ARC | OWN |

### Design Phase — Owner's Ongoing Responsibilities
- Attend design review meetings (bi-weekly minimum)
- Provide timely decisions on materials, finishes, equipment
- Approve design at each gate (SD → DD → CD) within agreed timelines
- Manage scope against budget (approve or reject additions)
- Provide brand standards and equipment specs to architect

**Warning:** Every week of owner decision delay = approximately one week of project delay.

## Phase 4: Entitlements & Permitting

> **Typical Duration:** 4-12+ weeks (building permit); 2-12+ months (entitlements)
> **See:** [[04 - Entitlements & Permitting]]

### Building Permit Task Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Submit permit application with CDs and fees | PM, ARC | PM | — | OWN, AHJ |
| 1 | AHJ completeness check | AHJ | AHJ | — | PM |
| 2-6 | Plan review by all AHJ disciplines | AHJ | AHJ | — | PM |
| 4-8 | Plan review comments issued | AHJ | AHJ | — | PM, ARC |
| 5-9 | Design team responds to comments | ARC, ENG | ARC | PM | AHJ |
| 5-9 | Resubmit revised drawings | ARC | PM | — | AHJ |
| 7-11 | Re-review by AHJ | AHJ | AHJ | — | PM |
| 8-12 | Permit approved; fees calculated | AHJ | AHJ | — | PM, OWN |
| 9-12 | Pay permit fees and impact fees | OWN | PM | — | OWN |
| 9-12 | **Permit issued** | AHJ | AHJ | — | All |

### Key Roles During Permitting
- **PM:** Tracks status, expedites, coordinates responses, pays fees
- **ARC/ENG:** Responds to technical comments, revises drawings
- **OWN:** Authorizes fee payments, signs applications (some jurisdictions require owner signature)
- **AHJ:** Reviews, comments, approves — PM's job is to make their job easy
- **Permit Expediter (if engaged):** Manages submissions, tracks status, maintains AHJ relationships

## Phase 5: Procurement & Bidding

> **Typical Duration:** 4-12 weeks
> **See:** [[05 - Procurement & Bidding]]

### DBB Procurement Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Assemble bid documents (drawings, specs, front-end) | ARC, PM | PM | ATT | OWN |
| 1 | Identify and pre-qualify bidders (3-5 GCs minimum) | PM | PM | OWN | — |
| 1-2 | Issue bid documents to qualified GCs | PM | PM | — | OWN |
| 2 | Pre-bid meeting / site walkthrough | PM | PM | ARC, OWN | GC (bidders) |
| 2-3 | Bidders submit RFIs during bid period | GC (bidders) | — | ARC | PM |
| 2-3 | Issue addenda responding to bid RFIs | ARC | PM | — | GC (bidders) |
| 3-4 | **Bids due** | GC (bidders) | — | — | PM |
| 4-5 | Bid evaluation (leveling, scope review, reference checks) | PM | PM | ARC, OWN | — |
| 5-6 | Contractor interviews (if needed) | PM | OWN | ARC | — |
| 5-6 | **Owner selects contractor** | OWN | OWN | PM, ARC | — |
| 6-8 | Contract negotiation | PM, ATT | OWN | ARC | GC |
| 7-10 | Insurance and bonding procurement | GC | GC | PM | OWN |
| 7-10 | SOV development and approval | GC | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 8-12 | **Contract executed** | OWN, GC | OWN | PM, ATT | All |

### CMAR Procurement Sequence (Different Timing)

| When | Task | Responsible | Accountable |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|
| During SD | RFQ/RFP for CM; CM selected on qualifications | PM | OWN |
| During DD | CM provides preconstruction services (cost, schedule, constructability) | CM | CM |
| Late DD / Early CD | GMP proposal developed by CM | CM | CM |
| During CD | Trade packages bid by CM to subcontractors (open-book) | CM | CM |
| CD Complete | GMP finalized and contract executed | CM, OWN | OWN |

### Long-Lead Procurement (Parallel Track)

| Week (from CD issue) | Task | Responsible | Accountable |
|---------------------|------|-------------|-------------|
| 1-2 | Identify long-lead items from schedule | GC/CM | PM |
| 2-4 | Submittals for long-lead items | GC/CM, SUB | GC/CM |
| 3-6 | Architect reviews long-lead submittals | ARC | ARC |
| 4-8 | Purchase orders placed for long-lead items | GC/CM | GC/CM |
| Ongoing | Track delivery dates weekly | GC/CM | PM |
