# Almonds & Fruit Mix

## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup raw almonds
- 1/4 cup dried cranberries
- 1/4 cup dried apricots, chopped
- 2 tbsp dark chocolate chips
- Pinch of sea salt

## Instructions
1. Combine almonds, dried cranberries, and dried apricots
2. Stir in dark chocolate chips
3. Add pinch of sea salt
4. Mix well
5. Portion into small containers or bags
6. Store in airtight container for up to 2 weeks

## Nutrition (per serving, 1/4 cup mix)
- Calories: 160
- Protein: 12g
- Carbs: 16g
- Fat: 6g
- Prep Time: 2 min
- Makes: 4 servings
