# Paint the Stock Tank Pool

## 📋 Overview
> *Transform the stock tank pool with a fresh, durable paint job that withstands water, sun, and Texas weather.*

**Objective:** Clean, prep, prime, and paint the stock tank pool with a waterproof, UV-resistant finish that looks great and lasts multiple seasons.

**Success Criteria:**
- Pool fully painted with even, smooth coverage (inside and out)
- No peeling, bubbling, or adhesion issues after 48-hour cure test
- Pool refilled and holding water with no paint degradation
- Completed by March 30, 2026

**Deadline:** March 30, 2026

> ⚠️ **Weather dependency:** Painting and priming phases need dry weather with temps above 50°F. Check forecast before starting Phase 4. If rain is expected, shift the schedule — better to delay than paint in moisture.

> 💡 **Pro tip:** Galvanized steel (what most stock tanks are) needs proper prep to hold paint. The TSP wash + sanding + bonding primer combo is non-negotiable — skip any of these and the paint will peel within weeks.
