# BM-Restaurant-Benchmarks — QSR & FSR Cost Reference

> Brand-by-brand and format-by-format benchmarks. All costs 2024 USD, national average.
> Apply [[BM-Regional-Cost-Index]] multiplier. Exclude FF&E, signage, utility connection fees unless noted.

---

## QSR — Quick Service Restaurant Benchmarks

### Chick-fil-A
| Format | SF | Hard Cost $/SF | Total Hard Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Freestanding (single DT) | 4,500–5,200 | $280–$420 | $1.3M–$2.2M | Brand-managed; proprietary specs; dual DT preferred |
| Freestanding (dual DT) | 4,800–5,800 | $320–$480 | $1.5M–$2.8M | Dual lane is current standard; major premium |
| End-cap TI | 3,000–4,000 | $220–$340 | $660K–$1.4M | No site work; existing shell |

**Key cost drivers:** Extensive landscaping and site lighting above QSR average; proprietary vendor requirements; dual-lane drive-thru technology package ($100K–$180K); premium kitchen package; high finish level in dining. Chick-fil-A often owns and builds their own buildings — hard costs reflect corporate construction standards.

---

### Panera Bread
| Format | SF | Hard Cost $/SF | Total Hard Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Inline TI (standard) | 3,500–4,500 | $160–$240 | $560K–$1.1M | Standard bread co. package |
| End-cap TI | 3,500–4,500 | $180–$270 | $630K–$1.2M | Drive-thru adds $120K–$200K |
| Renovation / brand refresh | 3,500–4,500 | $90–$160 | $315K–$720K | Existing Panera; partial demo and refresh |

**Key cost drivers:** Hearth oven requires dedicated gas service, structural slab reinforcement, dedicated ventilation; digital ordering infrastructure (kiosks, digital menu boards) requires extensive low-voltage rough-in; bread program requires dedicated dry storage with specific HVAC. Current brand standard includes significant millwork/casework investment.

### Starbucks
| Format | SF | Hard Cost $/SF | Total Hard Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Drive-thru only (express) | 500–1,000 | $500–$800 | $250K–$800K | Very small footprint; high $/SF |
| Drive-thru with café | 1,500–2,200 | $280–$420 | $420K–$924K | Current dominant prototype |
| Café only (inline) | 1,200–2,000 | $220–$350 | $264K–$700K | Urban/mall format |
| Reserve / Roastery | 3,000–8,000 | $450–$800 | $1.35M–$6.4M | Premium flagship; custom everything |

**Key cost drivers:** Starbucks Design & Construction manages specifications tightly; bar equipment rough-in (plumbing-dense); drive-thru communication and technology system; specific millwork standards; custom lighting packages; brand-required sustainable materials add cost. Starbucks often has a dedicated regional construction team managing vendor relationships.

## Restaurant Budget Allocation Quick Reference

For a 2,500 SF QSR TI (no drive-thru):

| System | % | $ Range |
|---|---|---|
| Demo + slab work | 4% | $18K–$32K |
| Concrete / slab core | 3% | $14K–$25K |
| Framing + rough carpentry | 5% | $23K–$40K |
| Finishes (Div 09) | 13% | $59K–$104K |
| Doors / storefront | 5% | $23K–$40K |
| Millwork / casework | 6% | $27K–$48K |
| Plumbing | 12% | $54K–$96K |
| HVAC | 17% | $77K–$136K |
| Electrical | 14% | $63K–$112K |
| Fire suppression | 4% | $18K–$32K |
| Fire alarm | 3% | $14K–$24K |
| Low voltage | 4% | $18K–$32K |
| GC general conditions | 10% | $45K–$80K |
| O&P | 15% | $68K–$120K |
| **Total** | **100%** | **$520K–$921K** |

*$/SF: $208–$368/SF on 2,500 SF*

### Sporting Goods / Outdoor / Active
*REI, Dick's, Academy, Bass Pro tier*

| Format | SF | $/SF Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| REI (prototype) | 15,000–45,000 | $120–$200 | Premium finishes; natural materials; complex ceiling |
| Dick's Sporting Goods | 40,000–80,000 | $80–$130 | More standard; high clearance |
| Academy Sports | 60,000–100,000 | $65–$100 | Value format; warehouse-adjacent |
| Specialty outdoor (small format) | 2,000–8,000 | $110–$175 | Boutique; higher $/SF |

**REI-specific cost premiums:**
- Exposed wood ceiling elements: +$8–$18/SF vs. standard ACT
- Climbing wall (if included): $80,000–$250,000 depending on size
- Café / espresso bar: $60,000–$120,000 infra
- Sustainability features (LEED certification target): +$5–$15/SF

## Retail Finish Tier Cost Impact

Finish specification level can double or triple the finishes budget:

| Tier | Flooring | Ceiling | Walls | Lighting | $/SF Total Finishes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Value | VCT / scored concrete | ACT 2×4 | Paint | Fluorescent/basic LED | $18–$30 |
| Standard | LVT or carpet tile | ACT with some accent | Paint + feature wall | LED retrofit/troffers | $28–$45 |
| Premium (REI, Target) | Polished concrete / LVT blend | Open-to-structure / custom ACT | Wood panel, graphic wall | Track + pendant LED | $40–$70 |
| Luxury | Natural stone / hardwood | GWB with coffers | Custom millwork, stone | Designer fixtures | $90–$200+ |
