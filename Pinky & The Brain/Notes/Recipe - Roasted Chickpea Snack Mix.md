# Roasted Chickpea Snack Mix

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 30 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 4 (1/2 cup per serving)
**Protein per Serving:** 24g
**Calories per Serving:** 280

---

## Ingredients

- 2 cans (800g total) chickpeas, drained, rinsed, dried thoroughly
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 1/4 cup unsalted pumpkin seeds
- 1/4 cup unsalted sunflower seeds
- 1/4 cup raw almonds
- 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 1/2 tsp sea salt
- Black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** — 375°F
2. **Dry chickpeas** — pat very dry with paper towels (moisture = won't crisp)
3. **Toss with oil and spices** — coat chickpeas with oil, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, nutritional yeast, salt, pepper
4. **Spread on baking sheet** — single layer
5. **Roast** — 30 min, shaking pan every 10 min for even browning
6. **Cool completely** — will crisp up as it cools (optional: spread on fresh paper towels)
7. **Mix with seeds and nuts** — toss in pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, almonds
8. **Store** — airtight container up to 5 days (or freeze up to 2 months)

## Notes

- **Crispy trick** — don't skip drying step, moisture kills crispness
- **Batch make** — triple recipe, store in mason jars
- **Meal-prep snack** — grab a handful pre-workout or between meals
- **Shopping overlap:** Chickpeas, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds used across recipes
