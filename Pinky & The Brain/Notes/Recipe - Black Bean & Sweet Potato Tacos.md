# Black Bean & Sweet Potato Tacos

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Mexican
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 35 min
**Servings:** 2 (3 tacos each)
**Protein per Serving:** 42g
**Calories per Serving:** 580

---

## Ingredients

### Filling
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced (1/2-inch cubes)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1/4 cup salsa
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Taco Assembly
- 6 small corn tortillas (or whole wheat)
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedges
- Hot sauce
- Vegan crema or cashew cream (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Roast sweet potatoes** — toss with 1 tbsp oil, salt, pepper; roast at 425°F for 20 min until crispy-edged
2. **Warm beans** — heat remaining oil in skillet, sauté onion and garlic 2 min
3. **Add spices** — cumin, chili powder, cayenne, cook 30 seconds
4. **Add beans** — stir in black beans, salsa, nutritional yeast, cook 3 min
5. **Warm tortillas** — heat over open flame or skillet
6. **Assemble** — beans base, top with roasted sweet potato, avocado, cabbage, cilantro
7. **Serve** — lime wedge, hot sauce, crema

## Notes

- **Roasted sweet potato** — adds natural sweetness, creamy texture, complex carbs for energy
- **Protein sources** — black beans (18g) + nutritional yeast (8g) + avocado (4g) = 30g+ in filling
- **Meal prep** — roast sweet potatoes, cook beans morning-of, assemble fresh
- **Shopping overlap:** Sweet potatoes, black beans, onions, garlic, cilantro, lime
