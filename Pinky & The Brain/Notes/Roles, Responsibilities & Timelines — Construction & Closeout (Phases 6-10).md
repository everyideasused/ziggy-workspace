## Phase 6: Preconstruction

> **Typical Duration:** 2-8 weeks
> **See:** [[06 - Preconstruction]]

### Task Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Issue Notice to Proceed (NTP) | OWN / PM | OWN | — | GC, ARC |
| 1 | Preconstruction kick-off meeting | PM | PM | OWN, ARC, GC, key SUBs | All |
| 1 | Establish communication protocols (RFI, submittal, meeting cadence) | PM | PM | GC, ARC | OWN |
| 1 | Set up project management platform (Procore, Smartsheet, etc.) | PM | PM | GC | OWN |
| 1-2 | GC develops baseline construction schedule (CPM) | GC | GC | PM, SUBs | OWN, ARC |
| 1-2 | GC submits Schedule of Values (SOV) for approval | GC | GC | PM | OWN |
| 1-2 | PM reviews and approves SOV | PM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 1-2 | GC submits site logistics plan | GC / SUP | GC | PM | OWN, LL |
| 1-2 | GC submits project-specific safety plan | GC / SUP | GC | PM | OWN |
| 1-3 | GC submits submittal schedule (all required submittals with dates) | GC / PE | GC | ARC | PM |
| 1-3 | Coordinate utility shutdowns and temporary services | GC | GC | PM | OWN, LL |
| 2-3 | PM reviews and approves baseline schedule | PM | PM | OWN | ARC |
| 2-4 | Early submittals for long-lead items | GC / SUB | GC | ARC | PM |
| 2-4 | ARC reviews early submittals | ARC | ARC | ENG | GC, PM |
| 2-4 | GC mobilizes to site (trailer, temp power, fencing, erosion control) | GC / SUP | GC | PM | OWN |
| 3-4 | Surveyor stakes building layout | SUR | GC | ARC, ENG | PM |
| 3-4 | Safety orientation for all on-site workers | GC / SUP | GC | — | PM |
| 4-6 | **All preconstruction items complete; construction begins** | GC | PM | — | OWN |

### Key Handoffs
- **PM → GC:** NTP, permit set, owner-furnished specs
- **GC → PM:** Baseline schedule, SOV, safety plan, logistics plan, submittal schedule
- **GC → ARC:** First wave of submittals
- **ARC → GC:** Submittal reviews returned

## Phase 7: Construction Execution

> **Typical Duration:** 6 weeks – 24+ months depending on project type
> **See:** [[07 - Construction Execution]]

### Weekly Recurring Activities (Every Week During Construction)

| Day | Activity | Responsible | Accountable | Informed |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Mon | GC updates 3-week look-ahead schedule | GC / SUP | GC | PM |
| Mon | GC conducts weekly safety toolbox talk | GC / SUP | GC | PM |
| Mon-Fri | GC Superintendent on site managing daily operations | SUP | GC | PM |
| Mon-Fri | SUP prepares daily reports (weather, manpower, work completed, issues) | SUP | GC | PM |
| Tue/Wed | **OAC Meeting** (Owner-Architect-Contractor) | PM (facilitates) | PM | OWN, ARC, GC |
| Tue/Wed | RFI log review: status of open RFIs | PM | PM | ARC, GC |
| Tue/Wed | Submittal log review: status of pending submittals | PM | PM | ARC, GC |
| Tue/Wed | Change order log review: status of PCOs and COs | PM | PM | OWN, GC |
| Tue/Wed | Schedule update discussion: critical path, delays, recovery | GC | PM | OWN, ARC |
| Fri | GC distributes updated daily reports for the week | GC / PE | GC | PM |
| Monthly | Pay application submission and review cycle (see below) | GC, PM, ARC | PM | OWN |

### Monthly Pay Application Cycle

| Day of Month | Task | Responsible | Accountable |
|-------------|------|-------------|-------------|
| 25th (or per contract) | GC submits pay application (G702/G703) with lien waivers | GC | GC |
| 25th-5th | PM and ARC review pay app; verify % complete vs. field conditions | PM, ARC | PM |
| 5th-10th | PM certifies pay app (or returns with redlines) | PM | PM |
| 10th-15th | Owner approves and processes payment | OWN | OWN |
| 15th-30th | Payment issued to GC (net of retainage) | OWN | OWN |
| Within 7 days of receipt | GC pays subcontractors | GC | GC |

### RFI Process — Roles and Timelines
| Step | Responsible | Timeline Target |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| GC identifies question, drafts RFI | GC / PE | — |
| GC submits RFI to architect (via project platform) | GC / PE | As needed |
| Architect reviews; consults engineer if needed | ARC / ENG | 5-7 business days (target) |
| Architect issues response | ARC | Within response deadline |
| PM tracks RFI age; escalates overdue items | PM | Weekly at OAC |
| GC implements response; documents cost/time impact | GC | Immediately upon receipt |

### Change Order Process — Roles and Timelines
| Step | Responsible | Timeline Target |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Issue identified (field condition, owner request, design error) | GC, PM, ARC | — |
| PM logs PCO (Potential Change Order) | PM | Within 48 hours |
| Scope defined (ASI, RFI response, or owner directive) | ARC or OWN | 3-5 business days |
| GC solicits pricing from subcontractors | GC | 5-10 business days |
| GC submits COR (Change Order Request) with full backup | GC | Within 10 business days of scope definition |
| PM reviews pricing; negotiates if needed | PM | 5-7 business days |
| Owner approves or rejects | OWN | 5 business days (target) |
| Formal Change Order executed | PM, GC | Within 5 business days of approval |
| SOV updated; billed in next pay app | GC | Next billing cycle |

## Phase 8: Closeout & Turnover

> **Typical Duration:** 2-8 weeks
> **See:** [[08 - Closeout & Turnover]]

### Closeout Task Sequence

| Week | Task | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|------|------|-------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Architect declares substantial completion; issues AIA G704 | ARC | ARC | PM, GC | OWN |
| 1 | Joint punch list walkthrough | ARC, PM, OWN | PM | GC | — |
| 1 | GC distributes punch list to responsible subs | GC | GC | SUBs | PM |
| 1-2 | Schedule all AHJ final inspections | GC | GC | PM | AHJ |
| 1-3 | AHJ final inspections (building, fire, MEP) | AHJ | AHJ | GC | PM, OWN |
| 1-3 | Correct any inspection deficiencies | GC / SUBs | GC | — | PM, AHJ |
| 1-4 | Punch list corrections by subcontractors | SUBs | GC | — | PM, ARC |
| 2-4 | **Certificate of Occupancy issued** | AHJ | AHJ | — | All |
| 2-4 | GC compiles closeout documentation package | GC / PE | GC | SUBs | PM |
| 2-4 | As-built drawings compiled | GC | GC | ARC | PM |
| 2-4 | O&M manuals collected from all subs | GC / PE | GC | SUBs | PM |
| 2-4 | Warranty letters collected from all subs and manufacturers | GC / PE | GC | SUBs | PM |
| 2-6 | PM reviews closeout package for completeness | PM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 2-6 | Final lien waivers (unconditional) from GC and ALL subs | GC | GC | SUBs | PM, OWN |
| 3-6 | GC submits final pay application (including retainage request) | GC | GC | — | PM |
| 3-6 | PM reviews final pay app and retainage release | PM | PM | ARC | OWN |
| 4-6 | Owner approves final payment and retainage release | OWN | OWN | PM | GC |
| 4-6 | Owner training on building systems (HVAC, fire alarm, security) | GC / SUBs | GC | PM | OWN |
| 4-8 | **Formal project handover meeting** | PM | PM | OWN, GC | All |
| 4-8 | GC demobilizes from site | GC / SUP | GC | PM | OWN |
| 4-8 | Insurance transition: builder's risk → permanent property | OWN | OWN | PM | — |
| 4-8 | TIA final reconciliation and draw (if applicable) | PM | PM | OWN | LL |

## Phase 9: Post-Construction & Warranty

> **Typical Duration:** 1-2 years
> **See:** [[09 - Post-Construction & Warranty]]

### Warranty Period Roles

| Task | Responsible | Accountable | Timeline |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Respond to warranty claims from owner | GC → responsible SUB | GC | Ongoing (1-2 year period) |
| Track and document warranty issues | PM / OWN | OWN | Ongoing |
| Conduct 11-month warranty walkthrough | PM, OWN | PM | Month 11 |
| Document all deficiencies found at 11-month walk | PM | PM | Month 11 |
| GC corrects all warranty items before expiration | GC / SUBs | GC | Before month 12 |
| Conduct lessons learned session | PM | PM | Within 30 days of closeout |
| Document lessons learned and update knowledge base | PM | PM | Within 60 days of closeout |
