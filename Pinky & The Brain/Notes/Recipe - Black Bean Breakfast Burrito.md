# Black Bean Breakfast Burrito

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** Mexican
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Total:** 25 min
**Servings:** 4
**Protein per Serving:** 26g
**Calories per Serving:** 420

---

## Ingredients

- 200g extra-firm tofu, drained and crumbled
- 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 4 large whole wheat tortillas
- 1/2 cup salsa
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Hot sauce to taste
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. **Sauté veggies** — heat oil, cook onion and pepper 4 min, add garlic 1 min
2. **Add tofu** — crumble into pan, add cumin, chili powder, paprika, nutritional yeast
3. **Cook 5 min** — until tofu is lightly browned
4. **Add beans** — stir in black beans, cook 3 min until heated through
5. **Season** — salt, pepper, hot sauce
6. **Assemble** — warm tortillas, fill with mixture, add salsa and avocado
7. **Roll tightly** — fold sides in, roll from bottom
8. **Serve or freeze**

## Notes

- **Freezer king** — wrap individually in foil, freeze up to 3 months
- **Reheat:** Microwave 2-3 min from frozen, or oven at 375°F for 20 min
- **Sunday prep:** Make 8-12 burritos, freeze, eat all week
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, black beans, nutritional yeast, tortillas used in multiple recipes
