## Healthcare MEP Cost Benchmarks

| System | % of TI Hard Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Plumbing | 12–20% | Hand sinks every exam room; medical gas add |
| HVAC (ASHRAE 170) | 15–25% | High ACH; pressure relationships; AHUs |
| Electrical | 12–20% | Emergency power; isolated panels; nurse call |
| Medical gas | 3–8% | Zone valves, outlets, manifold |
| Fire suppression | 2–4% | |
| Fire alarm / nurse call | 3–6% | |
| **MEP Total** | **45–65%** | Healthcare MEP is always the dominant cost |

# BM-MEP-Rules-of-Thumb — Quick MEP Budget Validation

## By Project Type (% of TI Hard Cost)

| Project Type | Plumbing | HVAC | Electrical | Fire Suppression | Fire Alarm | Low Voltage | **MEP Total** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Standard office TI | 5–8% | 12–18% | 10–15% | 2–4% | 2–4% | 2–4% | **33–53%** |
| Retail (soft goods) | 4–7% | 10–16% | 10–16% | 2–4% | 2–3% | 2–3% | **30–49%** |
| Restaurant QSR | 10–15% | 14–20% | 12–18% | 3–5% | 2–3% | 3–5% | **44–66%** |
| Restaurant FSR w/ bar | 12–18% | 15–22% | 14–20% | 3–5% | 2–4% | 3–5% | **49–74%** |
| Medical office | 12–20% | 15–25% | 12–20% | 2–4% | 3–6% | 3–5% | **47–80%** |
| Dental | 14–22% | 12–20% | 12–18% | 2–4% | 2–4% | 2–4% | **44–72%** |
| Ambulatory surgery | 14–20% | 18–28% | 14–22% | 2–4% | 4–8% | 3–6% | **55–88%** |

**If your MEP total falls outside the range for the project type — stop and audit.**
