# Vegetarian Lentil Pasta

## Ingredients
- 8 oz whole grain pasta
- 1 cup cooked lentils (or 1 can, drained)
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 2 cups spinach
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package directions, drain and set aside
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot
3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft (about 3 minutes)
4. Stir in tomato paste, cook 1 minute
5. Add diced tomatoes, lentils, and Italian seasoning
6. Simmer for 10 minutes
7. Stir in spinach until wilted
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Combine pasta with sauce and toss
10. Serve with fresh basil if desired

## Nutrition
- Calories: 380
- Protein: 18g
- Carbs: 62g
- Fat: 4g
- Prep Time: 25 min
- Serves: 2
