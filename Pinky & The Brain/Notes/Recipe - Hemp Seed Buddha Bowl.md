# Hemp Seed Buddha Bowl

**Meal:** Lunch
**Cuisine:** American / Bowl
**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 10 min
**Servings:** 1
**Protein per Serving:** 35g
**Calories per Serving:** 580

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 cup mixed greens
- 1/2 cup roasted chickpeas (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- 1 cup shredded carrots and beets
- 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
- 1/4 cup raw hemp seeds
- 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- 3 tbsp water
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Make dressing** — whisk tahini, lemon juice, garlic, maple syrup, water, salt, pepper
2. **Assemble bowl** — quinoa base, arrange all veggies
3. **Top with seeds** — hemp seeds and pumpkin seeds
4. **Drizzle dressing** — pour tahini dressing over top
5. **Eat immediately** or pack for lunch

- **Hemp seeds** — complete protein with all 9 amino acids (11g per 3 tbsp)
- **Prep tip:** Cook quinoa in bulk Sunday, chop veggies, assemble fresh each morning
