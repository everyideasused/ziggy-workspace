## 💡 TRAVEL AGENT INSIGHTS

### The Real Talk on Jazz Fest Week

1. **Pricing:** Expect 2-3x normal rates. That "reasonably priced" listing is probably not available April 22-27.

2. **Availability:** By March 2, the best stuff is 60-70% booked. The cream rises first (and gets reserved first).

3. **Location Trade-offs:**
   - **In the Quarter:** Walk home drunk safely, but noisy at night
   - **CBD (0.3mi away):** Quieter sleep, still walkable, often newer buildings
   - **Marigny/St. Roch (0.5mi):** Local vibe, Frenchmen Street music scene, cheaper

4. **The Hot Tub Question:**
   - Outdoor hot tub in April NOLA = potentially perfect temps (70s-80s)
   - Ask hosts if it's heated year-round or seasonal
   - Private > shared (obviously)

5. **Clawfoot Tub Reality:**
   - More common in historic Airbnbs
   - Romantic, but you're taking baths in humid weather
   - Hot tub > clawfoot for post-Jazz Fest sore feet
