# Monday Lower — Phase 1

**Focus:** Lower body strength, hip hinge mastery, leg hypertrophy  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Equipment:** Kettlebell (6-12 kg), pull-up bar, bodyweight

---

## Warm-up (5 min)

1. **Cat-Cow** — 10 reps  
   *Purpose:* Spinal mobility, activate core

2. **Hip Circles** — 10 each direction  
   *Purpose:* Hip mobility, glute activation

3. **Bodyweight Squat** — 10 reps  
   *Purpose:* Movement prep, joint lubrication

4. **Glute Bridge** — 15 reps  
   *Purpose:* Glute activation, hip extension

5. **Kettlebell Deadlift (light)** — 10 reps @ 6 kg  
   *Purpose:* Hinge pattern practice, CNS activation

---

## Main Work (20 min)

### A1. Goblet Squat — 3 sets × 8-12 reps
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Weight:** Start 6 kg, progress to 8 kg when 12 reps clean  
**Form cues:**
- Kettlebell at chest, elbows inside knees
- Squat to depth (hip crease below knee)
- Knees track over toes
- Chest up, core braced
- Drive through heels

**Progression:**
- Week 1-2: 6 kg × 3 sets × 8 reps
- Week 3-4: 6 kg × 3 sets × 10-12 reps
- Week 5-6: 8 kg × 3 sets × 8 reps
- Week 7-8: 8 kg × 3 sets × 10-12 reps
- Week 9-10: 10 kg × 3 sets × 8 reps
- Week 11-12: 10 kg × 3 sets × 10-12 reps

---

### A2. Kettlebell Swing — 3 sets × 15-20 reps
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Weight:** Start 8 kg, progress to 10 kg, then 12 kg  
**Form cues:**
- Hip hinge, NOT squat
- Kettlebell stays above knees
- Explosive hip snap at top
- Arms are ropes, hips do the work
- Float to shoulder height, control descent

**Progression:**
- Week 1-2: 8 kg × 3 sets × 15 reps
- Week 3-4: 8 kg × 3 sets × 20 reps
- Week 5-6: 10 kg × 3 sets × 15 reps
- Week 7-8: 10 kg × 3 sets × 20 reps
- Week 9-10: 12 kg × 3 sets × 15 reps
- Week 11-12: 12 kg × 3 sets × 20 reps

---

### B. Bulgarian Split Squat — 3 sets × 8-10 reps per leg
**Rest:** 60 seconds between legs  
**Weight:** Bodyweight → 6 kg goblet → 8 kg goblet  
**Form cues:**
- Rear foot elevated on bench/step (12-18 inches)
- Front shin vertical, knee tracks over toes
- Lower until rear knee nearly touches ground
- Drive through front heel
- Torso upright

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: Bodyweight × 3 sets × 8 reps per leg
- Week 5-8: 6 kg goblet × 3 sets × 8-10 reps per leg
- Week 9-12: 8 kg goblet × 3 sets × 8-10 reps per leg

---

### C. Single-Leg Deadlift — 2 sets × 8 reps per leg
**Rest:** 60 seconds between legs  
**Weight:** Bodyweight → 6 kg → 8 kg  
**Form cues:**
- Balance on one leg, slight knee bend
- Hinge at hip, reach opposite hand to floor
- Back leg extends behind (straight line from head to heel)
- Squeeze glute to return to standing
- Core tight, don't rotate

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: Bodyweight × 2 sets × 8 reps per leg
- Week 5-8: 6 kg (in working hand) × 2 sets × 8 reps per leg
- Week 9-12: 8 kg × 2 sets × 8 reps per leg

---

## Finisher (5 min)

### Kettlebell Farmer Carry — 3 sets × 30 seconds per side
**Weight:** 8-12 kg  
**Form cues:**
- Single kettlebell in one hand
- Walk with perfect posture (shoulders back, core braced)
- Don't lean away from weight
- Squeeze handle hard

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: 8 kg × 30 sec per side
- Week 5-8: 10 kg × 30 sec per side
- Week 9-12: 12 kg × 30 sec per side

## Post-Workout Stretching (10 min) — Lower Body Focus

**See:** [[Nathan 170@12 — Stretching Protocol#Monday — Post-Lower Body (10 min)|Full stretching guide]]

**Hold each stretch 30-60 seconds. Breathe deeply. Pain-free range only.**

1. **Hip Flexor Stretch (Couch Stretch)** — 60 sec per leg
   - Rear knee down, shin against wall/couch
   - Squeeze glute, drive hips forward
   - Feel stretch in front of hip

2. **Pigeon Pose** — 60 sec per leg
   - Front shin parallel to mat
   - Fold forward over front leg
   - Feel stretch in glute

3. **Seated Forward Fold** — 90 sec
   - Legs extended, hinge at hips
   - Reach toward toes
   - Feel stretch in hamstrings

4. **Quad Stretch (Standing)** — 60 sec per leg
   - Pull foot to glute
   - Keep knees together
   - Feel stretch down front of thigh

5. **Calf Stretch (Wall Lean)** — 60 sec per leg
   - Rear leg straight, heel down
   - Lean into wall
   - Feel stretch in calf

6. **90/90 Hip Stretch** — 60 sec per side
   - Sit with both legs at 90°
   - Lean over front leg
   - Feel stretch in hips

**Total:** ~9 minutes stretching + transition time
