# Chickpea Flour Omelette

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** French / Mediterranean
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 8 min | **Total:** 13 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 26g
**Calories per Serving:** 380

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
- 1 cup water
- 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp black salt (kala namak)
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp olive oil

### Filling
- 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 cup baby spinach
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- 2 tbsp vegan cheese (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Make batter** — whisk chickpea flour, water, nutritional yeast, turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, black salt, baking powder until smooth
2. **Rest 5 min** — let batter thicken slightly
3. **Cook filling** — sauté mushrooms in a bit of oil for 3 min, add spinach to wilt
4. **Heat pan** — medium heat, add olive oil to non-stick skillet
5. **Pour batter** — pour half the batter, swirl to coat pan evenly
6. **Cook 3-4 min** — until edges lift and bottom is golden
7. **Add filling** — place filling on one half
8. **Fold and serve** — fold omelette over, slide onto plate
9. **Repeat** — make second omelette with remaining batter

- **Chickpea flour** is a protein powerhouse — 21g per cup
- **Batter keeps** in fridge for 3 days — make ahead for quick mornings
- **Filling variations:** roasted peppers, olives, artichoke hearts
