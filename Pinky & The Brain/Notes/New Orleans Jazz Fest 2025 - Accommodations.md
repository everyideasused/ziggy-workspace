## Pro Tips for Jazz Fest Booking

### Timing & Pricing
- **Jazz Fest dates:** April 25-27 & May 2-4, 2025 (you're catching Weekend 1)
- **Book ASAP:** French Quarter fills up fast for Jazz Fest
- **Expect premium pricing:** 2-3x normal rates during festival weekends
- **Flexible on exact location?** Consider Faubourg Marigny or Bywater — walkable to French Quarter, better rates

### What to Look For
- **Walkability:** French Quarter parking is nightmare fuel during Jazz Fest
- **Balcony:** Request ground floor or balcony for people-watching
- **A/C:** April in NOLA = humid, make sure it's central A/C not window units
- **Noise:** French Quarter is loud; ask about street noise if you're light sleepers

### Alternative Neighborhoods (If FQ is booked/full budget)
1. **Faubourg Marigny** — Adjacent to FQ, Frenchmen Street music scene
2. **Bywater** — Artsy, 10-min Uber to Quarter
3. **Lower Garden District** — Historic, streetcar access
4. **CBD/Warehouse District** — Modern, walkable, often better amenities
