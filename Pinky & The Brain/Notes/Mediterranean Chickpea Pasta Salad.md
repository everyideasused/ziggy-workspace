## 📋 Ingredients

### Red Wine Vinaigrette
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tbsp red wine vinegar (or lemon juice)
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- Black pepper to taste

### Salad
- 8 oz pasta (farfalle, penne, or rotini) – use chickpea pasta for extra protein
- 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 10 oz fresh cherry or grape tomatoes, halved or diced
- 1 English cucumber, quartered and sliced
- 1/2 red onion, small diced
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives, halved (optional)
- 1/2-1 cup crumbled vegan feta
- 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint (optional)

---

## 👨‍🍳 Instructions

1. **Make dressing** – In a resealable jar, add oil, vinegar, garlic, mustard, oregano, salt, and pepper. Seal and shake vigorously until emulsified. Let sit; vinegar will mellow out the garlic over time. *(Make at least 30 min ahead if possible.)*

2. **Cook pasta** – Generously salt a large pot of water and bring to a boil. Cook pasta to al dente according to package directions, stirring occasionally. Drain and rinse well with cold water until pasta comes to room temperature. *Don't overcook!*

3. **Combine** – Add chilled pasta to large bowl along with chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, olives, feta, parsley, and mint. Pour dressing over top and toss until well combined.

4. **Serve** – Serve immediately, or refrigerate and chill for 30 minutes before serving for best flavor. Leftovers keep in fridge for up to 5 days.

---

## 📝 Notes
> *Tweaks, substitutions, what worked, what didn't.*

- **Al dente is key** – Don't overcook pasta! Tender with slight bite + cold rinse = perfect cold pasta salad. Overcooked pasta = mushy leftovers.
- **Make dressing ahead** – 30+ min ahead lets vinegar mellow the raw garlic. Big difference!
- **Chill for best flavor** – 30 min in fridge after assembly really helps flavors develop
- **More add-ins** – Sun-dried tomatoes, black olives, artichoke hearts, zucchini, spinach, bell peppers, pickled red onions, green onions, red pepper flakes
- **Extra protein** – Add crispy tofu, soy curls, or hemp seeds
- **Gluten-free** – Use GF pasta or chickpea pasta
- **Storage** – Keeps 5 days in airtight container in fridge. Do not freeze.
- **Protein boost** – Use chickpea pasta instead of regular for significantly more protein per serving
