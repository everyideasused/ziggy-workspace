# Quinoa Breakfast Bowl

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
- 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1/4 cup diced mango
- 1/4 cup blueberries
- 2 tbsp coconut flakes
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 banana, sliced
- 1 tsp honey

## Instructions
1. Warm cooked quinoa with almond milk in a saucepan
2. Transfer to a bowl
3. Top with mango, blueberries, and banana
4. Sprinkle coconut flakes and chia seeds
5. Drizzle with honey
