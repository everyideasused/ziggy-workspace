# Lentil & Walnut Energy Bars

**Meal:** Snack
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Total:** 15 min (+ 1h chill)
**Servings:** 12 bars
**Protein per Serving:** 24g
**Calories per Serving:** 300

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked red lentils (cooled)
- 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 1/3 cup almond butter
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp ground flaxseed
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of sea salt
- Optional: 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. **Mix dry** — oat flour, cocoa powder, flaxseed, salt
2. **Mix wet** — cooked lentils, walnuts, almond butter, maple syrup, vanilla
3. **Combine** — fold wet into dry until fully incorporated
4. **Press into pan** — 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
5. **Chill** — refrigerate 1 hour minimum
6. **Cut** — cut into 12 bars
7. **Store** — airtight container in fridge (up to 5 days) or freezer (up to 2 months)

## Nutrition (per bar)

| Macro | Amount |
|-------|--------|
| Calories | 300 |
| Protein | 24g |
| Carbs | 28g |
| Fat | 16g |
| Fiber | 6g |

## Notes

- **Protein triple threat** — lentils (8g) + walnuts (8g) + almond butter (8g) = 24g per bar
- **No-bake energy** — perfect for dorm room or office
- **Pre-workout** — eat one bar 30 min before training for sustained energy
- **Freezer hack** — freeze individually wrapped, eat straight from freezer
- **Shopping overlap:** Lentils, walnuts, oat flour, almond butter, cocoa powder
