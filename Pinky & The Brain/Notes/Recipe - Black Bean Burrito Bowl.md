# Black Bean Burrito Bowl

## Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- 1 cup black beans, cooked
- 1/4 cup corn
- 1/4 red bell pepper, diced
- 1/4 red onion, diced
- 1/4 cup diced tomato
- 1/4 avocado, sliced
- 2 tbsp salsa
- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt (or sour cream)
- Fresh cilantro
- Lime wedge

## Instructions
1. Warm rice in a pot or microwave
2. Heat black beans in a small pot
3. Place rice in a bowl
4. Top with black beans
5. Add corn, bell pepper, onion, and tomato
6. Place avocado slices on top
7. Dollop with Greek yogurt
8. Spoon salsa over the bowl
9. Garnish with cilantro and serve with lime wedge

## Nutrition

- Protein: 20g
- Carbs: 52g
- Fat: 6g
- Prep Time: 15 min
- Serves: 1
