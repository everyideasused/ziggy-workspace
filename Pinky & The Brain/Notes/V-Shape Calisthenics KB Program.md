# V-Shape Calisthenics + KB Program

## 📋 Overview
> *Build a bigger butt, pecs, shoulders, arms, and achieve a V-shape physique using bodyweight + kettlebell in 30-minute daily sessions.*

**Objective:** 150 lbs → 180 lbs lean bulk over 12–18 months. Shoulder-to-waist ratio > 1.45.

**Success Criteria:**
- Consistent 6 days/week training
- Protein compliance ≥ 5/7 days per week
- Weight gaining at 0.3–0.7 lbs/week
- Measurable strength progression every mesocycle
- Visible V-shape by Q2 (shoulder-to-waist ratio ≥ 1.40)

**Equipment:** Single kettlebell (starting 16kg) + pull-up bar
**Session Length:** 30 minutes
**Split:** Upper/Lower alternating, 4-week mesocycles

**Reference Docs:**
- [[V-Shape Program]] — Full program details, progression ladders, nutrition targets
- [[V-Shape Exercise Guide]] — Form cues, common mistakes, video links
- [[V-Shape Daily Logs]] — Workout log templates for each day type
