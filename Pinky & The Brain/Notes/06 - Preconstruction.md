# 06 - Preconstruction

> **Phase 6 of 10** | Predecessor: [[05 - Procurement & Bidding]] | Successor: [[07 - Construction Execution]]

---

## Purpose

Bridge between contract execution and the first day of physical construction. Establish all administrative, logistical, and planning frameworks needed for a successful build.

---

## Key Activities

### Administrative Setup
- Issue **Notice to Proceed (NTP)** — the formal authorization for the contractor to begin
- Conduct **preconstruction meeting** (owner, CM, GC, architect, key subs, AHJ if invited)
- Establish communication protocols: OAC meeting cadence, RFI process, submittal log, daily reports
- Set up project management software (Procore, Smartsheet, etc.)
- Establish **document control** procedures (drawing log, spec log, transmittal protocol)
- Confirm **billing procedures** — SOV approval, pay application schedule, lien waiver requirements

### Scheduling
- GC/CM develops the **baseline construction schedule** (CPM — Critical Path Method)
- Identify the **critical path** and key milestones
- Develop **3-week look-ahead schedules** (rolling short-interval schedules)
- Coordinate specialty sub schedules into master schedule
- Identify **schedule constraints** (weather, delivery, inspections, owner move-in)
- For schedule specification requirements and baseline review standards, see [[Schedule Specification & Analysis Methods]]

### Submittal Process Initiation
- GC submits **submittal schedule** (list of all submittals required with planned dates)
- Early submittals for long-lead items
- Architect reviews and approves/returns submittals
- **Shop drawings**, product data, samples, and mock-ups

### Site Logistics Planning
- Develop **site logistics plan** (staging areas, laydown, trailer locations, access points)
- Coordinate utility shutdowns and temporary services (power, water, sanitation)
- Traffic control plan (if impacting public roadways)
- Fencing, security, and site safety
- SWPPP (Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan) and erosion control

### Safety Planning
- GC develops **project-specific safety plan**
- Conduct safety orientation for all workers
- Establish emergency action plan
- Confirm OSHA compliance requirements
- Drug testing and background check protocols (if required by owner)

### Mobilization
- Contractor trailer and office setup
- Temporary utilities connection
- Material staging and initial deliveries
- Equipment mobilization
- Surveyor stakes building layout and key elevations

---

## Key Deliverables

- [ ] NTP issued
- [ ] Preconstruction meeting minutes
- [ ] Baseline construction schedule (approved)
- [ ] Submittal schedule
- [ ] Site logistics plan
- [ ] Safety plan
- [ ] Communication protocols document
- [ ] SOV approved
- [ ] Building permit posted on site

---

## Duration: 2-8 weeks depending on project complexity

---

## Transition to Next Phase

**Gate criteria to advance to [[07 - Construction Execution]]:**
1. NTP issued and acknowledged
2. Building permit in hand and posted
3. Baseline schedule approved by owner
4. Site mobilized, safety plan in place
5. Key submittals in process for early work
