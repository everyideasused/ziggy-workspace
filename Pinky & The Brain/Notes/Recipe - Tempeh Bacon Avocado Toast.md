# Tempeh Bacon Avocado Toast

**Meal:** Breakfast
**Cuisine:** American
**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Total:** 15 min
**Servings:** 2
**Protein per Serving:** 28g
**Calories per Serving:** 450

---

## Ingredients

- 1 block (225g) tempeh, sliced into thin strips
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for GF)
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 ripe avocado
- 4 slices whole grain bread, toasted
- 1 tbsp hemp seeds
- Red pepper flakes (optional)
- Lemon juice, squeeze

## Instructions

1. **Marinate tempeh** — toss strips with soy sauce, maple syrup, smoked paprika, garlic powder
2. **Heat oil** in skillet over medium-high heat
3. **Cook tempeh** — lay strips flat, cook 3-4 min per side until crispy and caramelized
4. **Prep avocado** — mash with lemon juice, salt, pepper
5. **Assemble** — spread avocado on toast, top with tempeh strips
6. **Garnish** — sprinkle hemp seeds and red pepper flakes
7. **Serve immediately**

## Notes

- **Tempeh bacon** stores in fridge for 5 days — batch cook on Sunday
- **Protein boost:** Add white beans mashed into the avocado (+8g)
- **Tempeh tip:** Steam for 10 min before marinating to remove bitterness
- **Shopping overlap:** Tempeh, soy sauce, hemp seeds used in multiple recipes
