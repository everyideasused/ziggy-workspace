# Friday Full Body — Phase 1

**Focus:** Full body integration, kettlebell complexes, work capacity  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Equipment:** Kettlebell (6-12 kg), pull-up bar, bodyweight

---

## Warm-up (5 min)

1. **World's Greatest Stretch** — 5 per side  
   *Purpose:* Hip mobility, thoracic rotation, full-body activation

2. **Inchworm + Push-up** — 5 reps  
   *Purpose:* Hamstring stretch, shoulder activation, core engagement

3. **Kettlebell Halo** — 10 each direction @ 6 kg  
   *Purpose:* Shoulder mobility, rotator cuff activation

4. **Jumping Jacks** — 30 reps  
   *Purpose:* Heart rate elevation, dynamic warm-up

---

## Main Work (20 min)

### A. Kettlebell Complex — 4 rounds
**Rest:** 2 minutes between rounds  
**Weight:** Start 8 kg, progress to 10 kg when form is perfect  
**Perform all 5 exercises back-to-back without setting kettlebell down:**

1. **Deadlift** × 5 reps  
2. **Clean (both sides)** × 3 reps per side  
3. **Front Squat** × 5 reps  
4. **Press (both sides)** × 3 reps per side  
5. **Swing** × 10 reps  

**Total reps per round:** 34 movements  
**Form cues:**
- Rest kettlebell on ground only between rounds
- Focus on smooth transitions
- Breathe between exercises
- If form breaks, rest and continue

**Progression:**
- Week 1-3: 8 kg × 4 rounds
- Week 4-6: 10 kg × 4 rounds
- Week 7-9: 12 kg × 4 rounds
- Week 10-12: 12 kg × 5 rounds (add 1 round)

---

### B. Pull-up Work — 4 sets
**Rest:** 90 seconds between sets  
**Progression based on current ability:**

**If you can do 2-4 pull-ups:**
- Set 1: Max strict pull-ups (aim for 3-5)
- Set 2: Max strict pull-ups (aim for 2-4)
- Set 3: Band-assisted pull-ups × 5
- Set 4: Negative pull-ups × 5 (5-second descent)

**If you can do 5-8 pull-ups:**
- 4 sets × Max reps (aim to add 1 rep total per week)

**Form cues:**
- Dead hang start, full lockout
- Pull chin over bar
- Control descent
- No kipping/swinging

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: Build to 5 strict pull-ups per set
- Week 5-8: Build to 8 strict pull-ups per set
- Week 9-12: Add weight or switch to wide-grip variation

---

### C. Core Tri-set — 3 rounds
**No rest between exercises, 60 sec rest between rounds**

**C1. Plank** — 30-60 seconds  
- Forearms on ground, body straight line  
- Don't sag hips, squeeze glutes  
- Breathe normally

**C2. Dead Bug** — 10 reps per side  
- Lie on back, arms up, knees bent 90°  
- Extend opposite arm/leg, return, switch  
- Keep lower back pressed to floor

**C3. Russian Twist** — 20 reps total (10 per side)  
- Sit with knees bent, lean back slightly  
- Hold kettlebell (6 kg), rotate side to side  
- Touch kettlebell to floor each side

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: Complete as written
- Week 5-8: Increase plank to 60-90 sec
- Week 9-12: Add 2 kg to Russian Twist, increase dead bug to 12 per side

---

## Finisher (5 min)

### Kettlebell Goblet Carry + Squat Ladder
**1 round, continuous work:**

- Goblet carry 20 steps → 1 goblet squat  
- Goblet carry 20 steps → 2 goblet squats  
- Goblet carry 20 steps → 3 goblet squats  
- Goblet carry 20 steps → 4 goblet squats  
- Goblet carry 20 steps → 5 goblet squats  

**Total:** 100 steps + 15 squats (should take ~3-4 minutes)  
**Weight:** 8-10 kg  
**Form:** Perfect posture on carries, full depth on squats

## Post-Workout Stretching (10 min) — Full Body

**See:** [[Nathan 170@12 — Stretching Protocol#Friday — Post-Full Body (10 min)|Full stretching guide]]

**Hold each stretch 30-90 seconds. Breathe deeply. Pain-free range only.**

1. **Child's Pose** — 60 sec
   - Rest position, deep breathing
   - Arms extended or by sides
   - Forehead to ground

2. **Cat-Cow Flow** — 10 reps (60 sec)
   - Hands and knees
   - Flow between arch (cow) and round (cat)
   - Inhale cow, exhale cat

3. **Downward Dog Hold** — 60 sec
   - Inverted V shape
   - Push hips up and back
   - Pedal feet to stretch calves

4. **Low Lunge (Hip Flexor + Quad)** — 60 sec per side
   - Front foot flat, rear knee down
   - Sink hips forward
   - Feel stretch in front of hip

5. **Lizard Pose** — 60 sec per side
   - Low lunge, front foot wide
   - Drop to elbows
   - Feel deep hip stretch

6. **Seated Spinal Twist** — 60 sec per side
   - Sit cross-legged
   - Twist to one side
   - Look over shoulder

7. **Supine Figure-4 (Glute Stretch)** — 60 sec per side
   - Lie on back
   - Cross ankle over opposite knee
   - Pull knee toward chest

8. **Corpse Pose (Savasana)** — 90 sec
   - Lie flat, arms by sides
   - Close eyes, body scan
   - Full body relaxation

**Total:** ~10 minutes stretching + transition time
