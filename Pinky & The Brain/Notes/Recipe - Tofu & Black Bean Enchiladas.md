# Tofu & Black Bean Enchiladas

**Meal:** Dinner
**Cuisine:** Mexican
**Prep Time:** 15 min | **Cook Time:** 25 min | **Total:** 40 min
**Servings:** 4 (2 enchiladas each)
**Protein per Serving:** 42g
**Calories per Serving:** 580

---

## Ingredients

### Filling
- 400g extra-firm tofu, drained and crumbled
- 1 can (400g) black beans, drained
- 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeño, minced (optional)
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 cup salsa (divided)
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper

### Sauce & Assembly
- 2 cups red enchilada sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- 8 corn tortillas
- 1 cup vegan cheese (optional, 60 cal)
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. **Make filling** — heat skillet, sauté onion and garlic 2 min, add crumbled tofu, black beans, corn, jalapeño
2. **Season** — add cumin, chili powder, nutritional yeast, 1/2 cup salsa, salt, pepper; cook 3 min
3. **Warm tortillas** — heat to make pliable
4. **Assemble** — spread thin layer of sauce on baking dish, fill each tortilla, roll tight, place seam-down
5. **Cover with sauce** — pour remaining sauce over enchiladas
6. **Bake** — 375°F for 20 min (add cheese if using, bake 5 more min)
7. **Garnish** — cilantro, lime wedge, extra salsa

## Notes

- **Makes 4 servings** — freeze half, eat fresh, reheat frozen one week later
- **Flavor boost** — fresh cilantro, lime, hot sauce essential at table
- **Shopping overlap:** Tofu, black beans, corn, cumin, nutritional yeast staples
