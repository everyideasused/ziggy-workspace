# Wednesday Upper — Phase 1

**Focus:** Upper body strength, pushing/pulling balance, shoulder health  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Equipment:** Kettlebell (6-12 kg), pull-up bar, resistance bands

---

## Warm-up (5 min)

1. **Arm Circles** — 10 forward, 10 backward  
   *Purpose:* Shoulder mobility

2. **Band Pull-Aparts** — 15 reps  
   *Purpose:* Rear delt activation, scapular retraction

3. **Scapular Push-ups** — 10 reps  
   *Purpose:* Serratus activation, shoulder stability

4. **Dead Hang** — 2 × 15 seconds  
   *Purpose:* Grip strength, shoulder decompression

5. **Push-up (easy variation)** — 10 reps  
   *Purpose:* Movement prep, chest/tricep activation

---

## Main Work (20 min)

### A1. Push-up Progression — 3 sets × Max reps (8-15 target)
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Variation:** Choose hardest version you can do 8+ reps  
**Form cues:**
- Hands shoulder-width, elbows 45° from body
- Body in straight line (plank position)
- Lower chest to floor (or as low as possible)
- Press up explosively
- Core tight, don't sag hips

**Progressions (choose your level):**
1. **Incline push-up** (hands on bench) — Weeks 1-4
2. **Knee push-up** (knees on ground) — Transition
3. **Full push-up** — Weeks 5-12
4. **Feet elevated push-up** — Advanced

**Week-by-week:**
- Week 1-2: 3 sets × 8+ reps (choose variation)
- Week 3-4: 3 sets × 12+ reps (same variation)
- Week 5+: Progress to harder variation when you hit 15 reps

---

### A2. Kettlebell Row — 3 sets × 10-12 reps per arm
**Rest:** 90 seconds  
**Weight:** Start 8 kg, progress to 10 kg, then 12 kg  
**Form cues:**
- Single-arm, opposite hand/knee on bench
- Back flat, core braced
- Row to hip, elbow close to body
- Squeeze shoulder blade back at top
- Control descent, don't swing

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: 8 kg × 3 sets × 10 reps per arm
- Week 5-8: 10 kg × 3 sets × 10 reps per arm
- Week 9-12: 12 kg × 3 sets × 12 reps per arm

---

### B. Kettlebell Overhead Press — 3 sets × 6-10 reps per arm
**Rest:** 60 seconds between arms  
**Weight:** Start 6 kg, progress to 8 kg, then 10 kg  
**Form cues:**
- Kettlebell in rack position (resting on forearm)
- Press straight overhead (bicep by ear at top)
- Don't lean back or arch excessively
- Lockout elbow completely
- Control descent

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: 6 kg × 3 sets × 6 reps per arm
- Week 5-8: 8 kg × 3 sets × 6-8 reps per arm
- Week 9-12: 10 kg × 3 sets × 8-10 reps per arm

---

### C. Pike Push-up — 2 sets × 8-12 reps
**Rest:** 60 seconds  
**Weight:** Bodyweight (shoulder focus)  
**Form cues:**
- Hands shoulder-width, hips high (inverted V shape)
- Lower head toward floor between hands
- Elbows point back, not out
- Press back to start
- This builds shoulder strength for handstands later

**Progression:**
- Week 1-4: 2 sets × 8 reps
- Week 5-8: 2 sets × 10 reps
- Week 9-12: 2 sets × 12 reps (or feet elevated for difficulty)

---

## Finisher (5 min)

### Superset: Bicep Curl + Tricep Extension
**3 rounds, no rest between exercises, 60 sec between rounds**

**A. Kettlebell Bicep Curl** — 10-12 reps  
- Weight: 6-8 kg  
- Both hands on kettlebell handle  
- Strict form, control eccentric

**B. Overhead Tricep Extension** — 10-12 reps  
- Weight: 6-8 kg  
- Kettlebell overhead, lower behind head  
- Elbows stay close to head

## Post-Workout Stretching (10 min) — Upper Body Focus

**See:** [[Nathan 170@12 — Stretching Protocol#Wednesday — Post-Upper Body (10 min)|Full stretching guide]]

**Hold each stretch 30-60 seconds. Breathe deeply. Pain-free range only.**

1. **Doorway Chest Stretch** — 60 sec per side
   - Arm on doorframe at 90°
   - Step through doorway
   - Feel stretch across chest

2. **Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch** — 60 sec per arm
   - Pull arm across chest
   - Opposite hand on elbow
   - Feel stretch in rear shoulder

3. **Lat Stretch (Overhead Side Bend)** — 60 sec per side
   - Reach arm overhead
   - Bend to opposite side
   - Feel stretch down side of torso

4. **Tricep Stretch (Overhead)** — 60 sec per arm
   - Reach overhead, bend elbow
   - Opposite hand pulls elbow deeper
   - Feel stretch down back of arm

5. **Thread the Needle** — 60 sec per side
   - Hands and knees position
   - Thread one arm under opposite arm
   - Feel stretch across upper back

6. **Child's Pose with Lat Focus** — 90 sec
   - Sit back on heels
   - Walk hands to one side (45 sec each)
   - Feel lat stretch

7. **Neck Stretch (4-way)** — 30 sec total
   - Ear to shoulder (left/right)
   - Chin to chest
   - Look up slightly

**Total:** ~10 minutes stretching + transition time
